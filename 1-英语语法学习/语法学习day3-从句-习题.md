# 语法学习 day3 - 从句习题

## 使用方式

本套题覆盖：从句原理、句子分类、名词性从句、定语从句、状语从句、从句语序、连接词选择。

### 2. 术语填空

用下面词语填空：`antecedent`、`relative pronoun`、`relative adverb`、`restrictive`、`non-restrictive`、`compound sentence`、`complex sentence`

1. 定语从句修饰的那个名词叫 __________。
2. `who / whom / which / that / whose` 叫 __________。
3. `where / when / why` 叫 __________。
4. 不能删，删掉后意思不完整的定语从句叫 __________ relative clause。
5. 有逗号，补充说明，删掉主句仍完整的定语从句叫 __________ relative clause。
6. 两个地位相同的简单句由 and/but/or/so 连接，叫 __________。
7. 主句 + 从句构成的句子叫 __________。

## 二、从句原理与句子分类

### 1. 判断句子类型

判断下列句子是简单句、并列复合句、主从复杂句。

1. The rabbit ate a carrot.
2. I saw that the rabbit ate a carrot.
3. The rabbit ate a carrot, and the wolf drank water.
4. When I arrived, the rabbit was eating.
5. The girl who is smiling is my friend.
6. I like coffee, but she likes tea.
7. To learn English takes time.
8. I know what he means.

### 2. 找出主句和从句

划出主句，并说明从句类型。

1. I believe that practice makes perfect.
2. What he said surprised everyone.
3. The reason why he left is unclear.
4. The book that I bought yesterday is useful.
5. Although it was raining, we went out.
6. The news that he won the prize made us happy.

## 三、名词性从句

### 1. 判断从句成分

判断划线部分是主语从句、宾语从句、表语从句、同位语从句、宾语补足语从句。

1. **That he passed the exam** is true.
2. I know **that you are busy**.
3. The problem is **that we have no time**.
4. I heard the news **that she won the prize**.
5. My education made me **who I am today**.
6. **Whether he will come** is still unknown.
7. I am sure **that there is a carrot on the table**.
8. His hope **that he could study abroad** came true.

### 2. 选择连接词

从 `that / whether / who / what / where / when / how` 中选择。

1. I don't know ______ he lives.
2. ______ he said is important.
3. The fact ______ he lied shocked us.
4. I wonder ______ he will come or not.
5. The question is ______ we can solve the problem.
6. It is clear ______ she is right.
7. Do you know ______ broke the window?
8. I remember ______ we first met.

### 3. 从句语序改错

改正下列宾语从句中的语序错误。

1. I don't know where does he live.
2. She asked what was I doing.
3. Can you tell me when will the train leave?
4. I wonder why did he refuse.
5. Do you know how can I improve my grammar?

### 4. 时态与否定转移

选择正确句子。

1. A. I don't think he is right.  
   B. I think he isn't right.
2. A. I knew the sun rose in the east.  
   B. I knew the sun rises in the east.
3. A. He said he was tired.  
   B. He said he is tired.
4. A. I am sure that he will come.  
   B. I am sure that will he come.

## 四、定语从句

### 1. 找出先行词、关系词、从句

1. The girl who wears red is my sister.
2. The carrot that I bought yesterday is fresh.
3. This is the place where we met.
4. I will never forget the day when I first learned grammar.
5. This is the reason why he was late.
6. The man whose car was stolen called the police.

### 2. 选择关系词

从 `who / whom / whose / which / that / where / when / why` 中选择。

1. The teacher ______ taught us grammar is patient.
2. The book ______ I bought is interesting.
3. The student ______ answer was correct smiled.
4. The city ______ I was born is beautiful.
5. The day ______ we met was cold.
6. The reason ______ he failed is simple.
7. The person ______ I saw yesterday was my old friend.
8. This is the best movie ______ I have ever seen.
9. My father, ______ is a doctor, works in Beijing.
10. He missed the train, ______ made him upset.

### 3. 限制性 or 非限制性

判断下列定语从句是限制性还是非限制性。

1. The students who studied hard passed the exam.
2. My mother, who is fifty, likes reading.
3. Beijing, which is the capital of China, is a large city.
4. The book that you recommended is helpful.
5. The rabbit ate the carrot, which was not surprising.

### 4. 合并句子

用定语从句合并。

1. I bought a book. The book is about grammar.
2. The man is my teacher. The man is standing near the door.
3. This is the house. I lived in the house ten years ago.
4. I remember the day. I met you on that day.
5. The girl is very kind. Her father is a doctor.

## 五、状语从句

### 1. 判断状语从句类型

判断是时间、原因、条件、让步、目的、结果、比较、方式、地点状语从句。

1. When I arrived, he was reading.
2. Because he was hungry, he ate the carrot.
3. If it rains tomorrow, we will stay at home.
4. Although he was tired, he kept studying.
5. I got up early so that I could catch the bus.
6. He was so tired that he fell asleep quickly.
7. She is as careful as her sister is.
8. Eat the carrot as I do.
9. The rabbit ate the carrot where he found it.
10. The more you practice, the better you become.

### 2. 选择连词

从 `before / when / while / since / until / as soon as / because / since / as / if / unless / although / even if / so that / so...that / than / as if / where` 中选择。

1. I will call you ______ I arrive.
2. He has studied English ______ he was ten.
3. We won't go out ______ it stops raining.
4. ______ you are busy, I will help you later.
5. ______ he is young, he knows a lot.
6. She speaks English ______ she were a native speaker.
7. I studied hard ______ I could pass the exam.
8. He ran faster ______ I did.
9. The rabbit was eating ______ the wolf came in.
10. You will fail ______ you work harder.

### 3. 主将从现

用括号中动词的正确形式填空。

1. If he ______ tomorrow, I will tell him. (come)
2. I will call you as soon as I ______ home. (get)
3. Unless it ______, we will play football. (rain)
4. The rabbit will eat the carrot when the wolf ______. (leave)
5. I will give you the book the next time I ______ you. (see)

### 4. 改错

1. Because he was hungry, so he ate a carrot.
2. Although it was raining, but we went out.
3. If the rabbit will see a carrot, he will eat it.
4. I don't know where does he live.
5. This is the place which I met him.
