## 九、连词

### 1. 并列连词

选择 `and / or / but / so / for / yet`。

1. I like tea, ______ she likes coffee.
2. Study hard, ______ you will fail.
3. It was late, ______ we went home.
4. He is poor, ______ he is honest.
5. She must be at home, ______ the light is on.

### 2. 从属连词

选择正确连词。

1. I stayed home ______ it was raining. (because / so)
2. ______ he is young, he is very independent. (Although / But)
3. I will call you ______ I arrive. (when / because)
4. He studies hard ______ he can pass the exam. (so that / such that)
5. You won't improve ______ you practice. (unless / because)

### 3. 改错

1. Because he was tired, so he slept early.
2. Although the test was difficult, but he passed.
3. He is smart, he is lazy.
4. I like English and she likes math.（两个完整分句，建议加逗号）
5. You can stay, or leave.（第二个动词共用主语）
