## 二、句子成分

### 1. 标出主要成分

标出主语、谓语动词、宾语、宾补、表语、定语、状语、同位语。

1. The teacher is kind.
2. He runs fast every morning.
3. She gave me a book.
4. We made him captain.
5. I saw her crossing the street.
6. The book on the desk is mine.
7. To learn English well is important.
8. What he said surprised everyone.
9. Beijing, the capital of China, is a big city.
10. The man in black bought a beautiful old car yesterday.

### 2. 判断五种基本句型

判断属于：S+Vi、S+Vt+O、S+Vt+IO+DO、S+Vt+O+OC、S+V+SC。

1. He sleeps well.
2. I like coffee.
3. She gave me a dictionary.
4. They elected him president.
5. The soup tastes delicious.
6. The sun rises.
7. I found the question difficult.
8. He became a teacher.
9. My father bought me a bike.
10. We discussed the problem.

### 3. 谓语 vs 谓语动词

分别写出“谓语动词”和“谓语”。

1. He runs fast.
2. She is reading a book.
3. They have finished the task.
4. You can solve the problem easily.
5. The news made her happy.

## 三、句子分类

### 1. 按结构分类

判断是简单句、并列复合句、主从复杂句。

1. I like English.
2. I like English, and she likes math.
3. When I arrived, he was cooking.
4. The man who called you is my teacher.
5. He studied hard, but he failed.
6. I know that he is right.

### 2. 按用途分类

判断是陈述句、一般疑问句、特殊疑问句、选择疑问句、反义疑问句、祈使句、感叹句。

1. She is a student.
2. Are you ready?
3. What are you doing?
4. Do you want tea or coffee?
5. You like grammar, don't you?
6. Please open the window.
7. What a beautiful sentence it is!

### 3. 句式转换

按要求改写。

1. She likes coffee.（改为否定句）
2. He can swim.（改为一般疑问句）
3. They went to Beijing yesterday.（对 Beijing 提问）
4. Open the door.（改为否定祈使句）
5. The flower is beautiful.（改为感叹句，用 How）

## 五、倒装

### 1. 判断倒装类型

判断是完全倒装、部分倒装、形式倒装。

1. Here comes the bus.
2. On the wall hangs a picture.
3. Never have I seen such a thing.
4. Only then did I understand the truth.
5. Should it rain tomorrow, we would stay home.
6. So fast did he run that nobody caught him.
7. The more you practice, the better you become.
8. Tired as he was, he kept working.

### 2. 改成倒装句

1. I have never seen such a beautiful view.
2. He realized the mistake only then.
3. If it should rain tomorrow, we would cancel the trip.
4. A tall tree stands in front of the house.
5. He was so tired that he could not speak.

## 六、强调

### 1. do/does/did 强调

用助动词强调谓语。

1. I like this book.
2. She knows the answer.
3. He told me the truth.
4. They need your help.
5. We finished the task yesterday.

### 2. it is/was...that 强调句

强调括号中的部分。

1. Tom met Mary in the park yesterday.（Tom）
2. Tom met Mary in the park yesterday.（Mary）
3. Tom met Mary in the park yesterday.（in the park）
4. Tom met Mary in the park yesterday.（yesterday）
5. I learned grammar because I wanted to improve my writing.（because 从句）

### 3. 判断是否为强调句

1. It is clear that he is right.
2. It was Tom that broke the window.
3. It is important to learn English.
4. It was yesterday that I met him.
5. It seems that she is tired.

## 八、主谓一致

7. The police ______ coming.  
   A. is B. are
8. Ten dollars ______ enough.  
   A. is B. are
9. A number of students ______ absent.  
   A. is B. are

   A. is B. are
