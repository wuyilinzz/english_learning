## 六、独立主格

### 1. 判断：从句还是独立主格

1. When the work was finished, we went home.
2. The work finished, we went home.
3. Because the weather was fine, we went out.
4. The weather being fine, we went out.
5. The teacher entered, book in hand.
6. If time permits, we will discuss it later.

### 2. 改写

把状语从句改成独立主格。

1. When the meeting was over, everyone left.
2. Because no one talked with him, he felt lonely.
3. If all things are considered, the plan is practical.
4. When the homework was finished, the students went out.
5. Because the weather was fine, we decided to climb the hill.
