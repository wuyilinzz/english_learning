# 1 句子成分 & 分类

> **核心牢记**：**谓语 ≠ 谓语动词**
> - **谓语动词**：指动词本身的形式（时态、语态、语气）
> - **谓语**：指整个谓部分，包括谓语动词及其宾语、补语等成分

## 1.1 句子成分

**定义**：句子的组成成分。英语句子的基本结构为：

> **主语 + 谓语 + （宾语） + （补语）**

一个完整的句子通常包含以下成分。

### 1.1.1 主语

**定义**：句子陈述的**对象**，即动作的执行者或状态的主体。回答 "Who?" 或 "What?" 的问题。

**位置**：通常位于句首。

**充当主语的词类**：

| 词类 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| 名词 | **The teacher** is kind. |
| 代词 | **He** is my friend. |
| 数词 | **Two** is enough. |
| 不定式 | **To learn English** is important. |
| 动名词 | **Running** is good for health. |
| 从句（主语从句） | **What he said** is true. |
| 名词短语 | **The man in black** is my uncle. |

**形式主语 it**：
- 当主语是不定式、动名词或从句时，常放在句尾，句首用 `it` 作形式主语。
  - **It** is important **to learn English**.
  - **It** is no use **crying**.
  - **It** seems that he is right.

### 1.1.2 谓语

**定义**：说明主语的**动作或状态**，是句子的核心。**谓语 ≠ 谓语动词**，谓语包括**谓语动词**及其后的宾语、补语等。

**结构**：谓语 = 谓语动词 + （宾语/补语/状语等）

**谓语与谓语动词的区别**：

| 概念 | 定义 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| 谓语动词 | 动词本身（有时态/语态/语气变化） | He **runs**. （runs 是谓语动词） |
| 谓语 | 整个谓部分，包括谓语动词及其连带成分 | He **runs fast**. （runs fast 是谓语） |

**谓语的类型**：

| 类型 | 结构 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| 动词谓语 | 实义动词 | He **runs** every day. |
| 系表谓语 | 系动词 + 表语 | She **is happy**. |
| 情态谓语 | 情态动词 + 动词原形 | You **can do** it. |

### 1.1.3 谓语动词

**定义**：谓语中的**核心动词**，体现**时态、语态、语气**，并受主语的人称和数的制约。

**谓语动词的构成**：

| 构成 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| 单个实义动词 | He **works** hard. |
| 助动词 + 实义动词 | He **is working** now. |
| 情态动词 + 实义动词 | He **can work** faster. |
| 助动词 + 情态动词 + 实义动词 | He **must have been working** all night. |

**谓语动词的五种形态**：

| 形态 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| 及物动词（需要宾语） | She **likes** coffee. |
| 不及物动词（不需要宾语） | He **sleeps** well. |
| 系动词（后接表语） | He **is** a teacher. |
| 双宾语动词（接间接宾语+直接宾语） | She **gave** me a book. |
| 复合宾语动词（接宾语+宾补） | We **made** him captain. |

### 1.1.4 宾语

**定义**：动作的**承受者**，回答 "Whom?" 或 "What?" 的问题。只有及物动词或介词才有宾语。

**类型**：

| 类型 | 定义 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| 直接宾语 | 动作的直接对象 | I bought **a car**. |
| 间接宾语 | 动作的间接对象（通常是人） | She gave **me** a book.（me 是间接宾语） |
| 介词宾语 | 介词后的名词/代词 | I look at **the picture**. |

**充当宾语的词类**：

| 词类 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| 名词 | I like **coffee**. |
| 代词 | I like **it**. |
| 数词 | I need **two**. |
| 不定式 | I want **to go**. |
| 动名词 | I enjoy **reading**. |
| 从句（宾语从句） | I know **that he is right**. |

### 1.1.5 宾语补足语

**定义**：放在宾语后面，补充说明宾语的**身份、特征、状态**。没有宾语就没有宾补。

**结构**：主语 + 谓语动词 + 宾语 + 宾补

**能带宾补的动词**：make, call, name, elect, find, keep, leave, see, hear, watch, consider, think, believe 等。

| 补足成分 | 示例 |
|----------|------|
| 名词 | They elected him **president**. |
| 形容词 | The news made her **sad**. |
| 不定式 | He asked me **to come**. |
| 现在分词 | I saw her **crossing the street**. |
| 过去分词 | I want the work **done** by Friday. |
| 介词短语 | She put the book **on the table**. |

### 1.1.6 主语补足语（表语）

**定义**：放在**系动词**之后，说明主语的**身份、特征、状态**。主补和表语是同一概念。

**系动词分类**：

| 系动词类型 | 示例 |
|------------|------|
| be 动词 | I **am** a student. |
| 感官动词 | It **sounds** good. / It **tastes** delicious. |
| 变化动词 | He **became** angry. / It **turned** red. |
| 保持动词 | Please **keep** quiet. / It **remains** a mystery. |
| 表象动词 | She **seems** tired. / He **appears** happy. |

**充当表语的词类**：

| 词类 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| 名词 | He is **a teacher**. |
| 形容词 | She is **beautiful**. |
| 代词 | The book is **mine**. |
| 数词 | We are **three**. |
| 副词 | The meeting is **over**. |
| 介词短语 | The book is **on the desk**. |
| 不定式 | My dream is **to travel the world**. |
| 动名词 | Her hobby is **painting**. |
| 从句（表语从句） | That is **what I mean**. |

### 1.1.7 定语

**定义**：修饰名词或代词，说明其**特征、属性、数量**等。

**位置**：

| 位置 | 说明 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| 前置定语 | 放在名词前 | a **red** apple / **three** books |
| 后置定语 | 放在名词后 | something **special** / the man **in black** |

**充当定语的词类**：

| 词类 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| 形容词 | a **beautiful** girl |
| 名词 | a **history** book / **school** bus |
| 代词 | **my** book / **that** car |
| 数词 | **three** apples / **the first** day |
| 介词短语 | the book **on the desk** |
| 不定式 | a plan **to study abroad** |
| 现在分词 | a **sleeping** baby |
| 过去分词 | **boiled** water |
| 从句（定语从句） | the man **who came yesterday** |

### 1.1.8 状语

**定义**：修饰动词、形容词、副词或整个句子，表示**时间、地点、原因、目的、结果、条件、方式、让步、程度、频率**等。

**状语分类**：

| 类别 | 常见状语 | 示例 |
|------|----------|------|
| 时间 | now, then, today, yesterday, soon | I will go **tomorrow**. |
| 地点 | here, there, everywhere, outside | He lives **here**. |
| 方式 | quickly, slowly, carefully, well | She sings **beautifully**. |
| 程度 | very, quite, too, so, extremely | It's **very** good. |
| 频率 | always, often, sometimes, never | He **often** comes late. |
| 原因 | because of, due to | He left **because of the rain**. |
| 目的 | to, in order to | He studied **to pass the exam**. |
| 条件 | if, unless | **If it rains**, stay home. |
| 让步 | although, though | **Although tired**, he kept working. |
| 伴随 | with | He came **with a smile**. |

**充当状语的词类**：

| 词类 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| 副词 | He runs **fast**. |
| 介词短语 | He lives **in Beijing**. |
| 名词短语 | He came **this morning**. |
| 不定式 | He went there **to see her**. |
| 分词 | **Tired**, he went home. |
| 从句（状语从句） | I will call you **when I arrive**. |

### 1.1.9 同位语

**定义**：放在名词或代词后面，对前者进行**解释、说明或补充**，与其指代同一事物。

**位置**：紧跟在被解释的词后面。

**类型**：

| 类型 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| 名词作同位语 | This is my friend **Tom**. |
| 名词短语作同位语 | Beijing, **the capital of China**, is a big city. |
| 数词作同位语 | We **two** will go. |
| 从句作同位语（同位语从句） | I heard the news **that she won the prize**. |

**同位语与定语的区别**：

| 对比项 | 同位语 | 定语 |
|--------|--------|------|
| 功能 | 解释说明，所指相同 | 修饰限定，范围缩小 |
| 关系 | 等同关系 | 从属关系 |
| 示例 | my friend **Tom**（Tom = my friend） | my friend **from Beijing**（不是等同） |

---

## 1.2 句子分类

### 1.2.1 按结构复杂度分类

#### 简单句

**定义**：只包含**一个主谓结构**的句子。

**五种基本句型**：

| 句型 | 结构 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| 主 + 谓 | S + Vi | He **runs**. |
| 主 + 谓 + 宾 | S + Vt + O | I **like coffee**. |
| 主 + 谓 + 宾 + 宾 | S + Vt + IO + DO | She **gave me a book**. |
| 主 + 谓 + 宾 + 宾补 | S + Vt + O + OC | We **made him captain**. |
| 主 + 系 + 主补 | S + V + SC | She **is beautiful**. |

**S** = Subject（主语）| **V** = Verb（动词）| **Vi** = 不及物动词 | **Vt** = 及物动词
**O** = Object（宾语）| **IO** = Indirect Object（间接宾语）| **DO** = Direct Object（直接宾语）
**OC** = Object Complement（宾语补足语）| **SC** = Subject Complement（主语补足语/表语）

#### 复合句（并列复合句）

**定义**：由**并列连词**连接两个或两个以上**语法地位相同**的简单句构成的句子。

**结构**：简单句 + 并列连词 + 简单句

**常见并列连词**：

| 逻辑关系 | 连词 | 示例 |
|----------|------|------|
| 并列/顺承 | and, not only...but also | I like coffee **and** she likes tea. |
| 选择 | or, either...or | You can stay **or** you can leave. |
| 转折 | but, yet | I tried hard **but** I failed. |
| 因果 | so, for | It rained **so** I stayed home. |

#### 复杂句（主从复合句）

**定义**：包含一个**主句**和一个或多个**从句**的句子。从句由**从属连词**引导，在句中充当某一句子成分。

**结构**：主句 + 从属连词 + 从句

**从句类型**：

| 从句类型 | 充当的成分 | 引导词示例 |
|----------|------------|------------|
| 主语从句 | 主语 | that, whether, what, who |
| 宾语从句 | 宾语 | that, whether, if, what |
| 表语从句 | 表语 | that, whether, what |
| 同位语从句 | 同位语 | that, whether |
| 定语从句 | 定语 | who, whom, which, that, whose |
| 状语从句 | 状语 | when, because, if, although |

**例句**：
- **When I arrived**（状语从句）, he **was cooking**（主句）.
- I **know**（主句谓语） **that he is right**（宾语从句）.

### 1.2.2 按用途分类

#### 陈述句

**定义**：陈述一个**事实**或**观点**，句末用句号。

**类型**：

| 类型 | 结构 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| 肯定陈述句 | 主语 + 谓语... | I like coffee. |
| 否定陈述句 | 主语 + 助动词/情态动词 + not + 谓语... | I do not like tea. / He cannot come. |

#### 疑问句

##### 一般疑问句

**定义**：用 **Yes** 或 **No** 回答的疑问句。

**结构**：助动词/情态动词/be 动词 + 主语 + 谓语...？

| 示例 | 回答 |
|------|------|
| **Do** you like coffee? | Yes, I do. / No, I don't. |
| **Is** she a teacher? | Yes, she is. / No, she isn't. |
| **Can** you swim? | Yes, I can. / No, I can't. |

##### 特殊疑问句

**定义**：用**疑问词**提问，询问具体信息的疑问句。

**结构**：疑问词 + 一般疑问句（语序）？

**常见疑问词**：

| 疑问词 | 提问内容 | 示例 |
|--------|----------|------|
| what | 事物 | What is your name? |
| who | 人（主语） | Who is that? |
| whom | 人（宾语） | Whom did you see? |
| whose | 所属 | Whose book is this? |
| which | 选择 | Which color do you like? |
| when | 时间 | When will you arrive? |
| where | 地点 | Where do you live? |
| why | 原因 | Why are you late? |
| how | 方式/程度 | How are you? |
| how many | 数量（可数） | How many books do you have? |
| how much | 数量（不可数）/价格 | How much is it? |
| how long | 时长 | How long will you stay? |
| how often | 频率 | How often do you exercise? |

##### 选择疑问句

**定义**：提供两个或以上选项，让对方选择的疑问句。

**结构**：一般疑问句 + or + 选项

**例句**：
- Do you like **coffee or tea**?
- Is it **Monday or Tuesday**?
- Will you go **by train or by plane**?

##### 反义疑问句

**定义**：陈述句后加一个简短的疑问句，用来**确认信息**或**寻求认同**。

**结构原则**：**前肯后否，前否后肯**

| 前句 | 后句 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| 肯定 | 否定 + 主语代词 | You like coffee, **don't you**? |
| 否定 | 肯定 + 主语代词 | You don't like tea, **do you**? |

**特殊情况的变体**：

| 前句情况 | 后句形式 | 示例 |
|----------|----------|------|
| 前句有否定词（never, hardly, seldom, few, little, no, nothing） | 肯定 | He never smokes, **does he**? |
| 前句是祈使句（Let's） | shall we | Let's go, **shall we**? |
| 前句是祈使句（其他） | will you | Open the door, **will you**? |
| 前句是 I am... | aren't I | I am late, **aren't I**? |
| 前句是 There be... | be + there | There is a book, **isn't there**? |
| 前句是 Everything/Anything/This/That | it | Everything is fine, **isn't it**? |
| 前句是 Everyone/Someone/No one | they | Everyone is here, **aren't they**? |

**回答方式**（用 yes/no 回答，与事实一致）：

| 事实 | 问题 | 回答 |
|------|------|------|
| 事实是喜欢 | You like coffee, don't you? | **Yes, I do.**（是的，我喜欢） |
| 事实是不喜欢 | You like coffee, don't you? | **No, I don't.**（不，我不喜欢） |
| 事实是喜欢 | You don't like tea, do you? | **Yes, I do.**（不，我喜欢） |
| 事实是不喜欢 | You don't like tea, do you? | **No, I don't.**（是的，我不喜欢） |

> **注意**：英语反义疑问句的回答是对**事实**的回答，而不是对问句形式的回答。

#### 祈使句

**定义**：表示**请求、命令、建议、劝告**等，主语 you 通常省略。

**结构**：

| 类型 | 结构 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| 肯定祈使句 | 动词原形 + ... | **Open** the door. |
| 否定祈使句 | Don't + 动词原形 + ... | **Don't** be late. |
| 以 Let 开头的祈使句 | Let + 宾语 + 动词原形 | **Let** me help you. / **Let's** go. |
| 礼貌祈使句 | Please + 祈使句 | Please sit down. |

#### 感叹句

**定义**：表达**强烈情感**（惊讶、喜悦、愤怒等）的句子，句末用感叹号。

**结构**：

| 结构 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| What + a/an + 形容词 + 可数名词单数 + （主语+谓语）！ | **What a beautiful day** (it is)! |
| What + 形容词 + 可数名词复数 + （主语+谓语）！ | **What beautiful flowers** (they are)! |
| What + 形容词 + 不可数名词 + （主语+谓语）！ | **What lovely weather** (it is)! |
| How + 形容词/副词 + （主语+谓语）！ | **How beautiful** (it is)! |
| How + 主语 + 谓语！ | **How time flies**! |

**what 与 how 的转换**：

| what 结构 | how 结构 |
|-----------|----------|
| What a beautiful girl she is! | How beautiful a girl she is! |
| What beautiful flowers they are! | How beautiful the flowers are! |

## 雅思考试中的句子成分与分类

1. **阅读部分**：
   - 快速识别主谓结构，排除枝叶成分（定语、状语）
   - 长难句分析：先找谓语动词，再找主语和宾语

2. **写作部分**：
   - 避免句子成分残缺（如无主句、缺少谓语）
   - 合理使用简单句、复合句和复杂句的搭配
   - 反义疑问句写作中较少使用，但要注意阅读中的理解

3. **口语部分**：
   - 祈使句用于给出建议：`Let me give you an example...`
   - 感叹句增强语气：`What a great idea!`

4. **语法基础**：
   - 牢记 **谓语 ≠ 谓语动词**，这是分析长难句的关键
   - 掌握五种基本句型是构建任何句子的基础

# 2 语态

**定义**：语态是动词的一种形式，用来表示**主语与谓语动词之间的关系**。

**语态分类总览**：

| 语态 | 定义 | 结构 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|------|
| 主动语态 | 主语是动作的**执行者** | 主语 + 及物动词 + 宾语 | The boy **kicked** the ball. |
| 被动语态 | 主语是动作的**承受者** | 主语 + be + 过去分词 + (by + 执行者) | The ball **was kicked** by the boy. |

> **核心记忆**：
> - 主动：主语 **做** 某事
> - 被动：主语 **被** 做

---

## 2.1 主动语态

**定义**：主语是动作的**发出者**或**执行者**。主动语态是英语中最常用、最自然的语态。

**结构公式**：
> **主语（执行者） + 谓语动词（主动形式） + 宾语（承受者）**

**例句**：
- **The chef** cooks the meal. （厨师做饭——厨师是动作发出者）
- **The company** launched a new product. （公司发布了新产品）
- **Millions of people** watch this show every week. （数百万人观看这个节目）

**与被动语态的对比**：
主动语态更**直接、简洁、有力**。在雅思写作和口语中，除非需要强调动作的承受者或不知道执行者是谁，否则优先使用主动语态。

---

## 2.2 被动语态

**定义**：主语是动作的**承受者**。当不知道、不重要、或故意不提及动作执行者时使用。

**结构公式**：
> **主语（承受者） + be + 过去分词 + (by + 执行者)**

| 句子成分 | 被动语态中的变化 |
|----------|------------------|
| 原宾语（承受者） | 变成 **主语** |
| 原主语（执行者） | 变成 by 的宾语（可省略） |
| 谓语动词 | 变成 **be + 过去分词** |

**转换示例**：

| 主动语态 | → | 被动语态 |
|----------|---|----------|
| The boy kicked **the ball**. | → | **The ball** was kicked (by the boy). |
| Shakespeare wrote **Hamlet**. | → | **Hamlet** was written by Shakespeare. |
| Someone stole **my wallet**. | → | **My wallet** was stolen. |

---

### 2.2.1 被动语态的构成（各时态）

> **公式**：`be + 过去分词`（be 动词体现时态和人称）

| 时态 | 构成 | 主动示例 | 被动示例 |
|------|------|----------|----------|
| 一般现在时 | am/is/are + done | The company **produces** cars. | Cars **are produced** by the company. |
| 一般过去时 | was/were + done | The chef **cooked** dinner. | Dinner **was cooked** by the chef. |
| 一般将来时 | will be + done | They **will build** a bridge. | A bridge **will be built**. |
| 现在进行时 | am/is/are being + done | They **are repairing** the road. | The road **is being repaired**. |
| 过去进行时 | was/were being + done | They **were painting** the house. | The house **was being painted**. |
| 现在完成时 | have/has been + done | Someone **has cleaned** the room. | The room **has been cleaned**. |
| 过去完成时 | had been + done | They **had finished** the project. | The project **had been finished**. |
| 将来完成时 | will have been + done | They **will have completed** it by May. | It **will have been completed** by May. |
| 情态动词 | 情态动词 + be + done | You **must write** the report today. | The report **must be written** today. |

> **口诀**：被动 be done 要记牢，时态人称看 be 的变化。

---

### 2.2.2 带双宾语动词的被动语态

**定义**：部分动词可以带两个宾语——**间接宾语**（通常指人）和**直接宾语**（通常指物）。变成被动语态时，可以有两种形式。

**常见动词**：give, show, teach, send, offer, tell, buy, make, ask, pay, promise 等。

**转换规则**：

| 类型 | 主动结构 | 被动结构 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 间接宾语变主语 | give **sb** sth | **sb** + be + given + sth |
| 直接宾语变主语 | give sb **sth** | **sth** + be + given + to sb |

**示例**（以 give 为例）：

| 主动 | He gave **me** a book. |
|------|------------------------|
| 被动（间接宾语作主语） | **I** was given a book (by him). |
| 被动（直接宾语作主语） | **A book** was given to me (by him). |

**更多示例**：

| 动词 | 主动 | 被动（人作主语） | 被动（物作主语） |
|------|------|------------------|------------------|
| tell | He told us a story. | We were told a story. | A story was told to us. |
| offer | They offered her a job. | She was offered a job. | A job was offered to her. |
| send | I sent him a package. | He was sent a package. | A package was sent to him. |

---

### 2.2.3 带复合宾语动词的被动语态

**定义**：部分动词可以带**宾语 + 宾语补足语**。变成被动语态时，原来的宾语变成主语，原来的宾补变成**主语补足语**。

**常见动词**：make, call, name, elect, choose, find, consider, think, believe 等。

**转换规则**：

| 主动结构 | 被动结构 |
|----------|----------|
| 主语 + 动词 + 宾语 + 宾补 | 原宾语 + be + 过去分词 + 原宾补（现主补） |

**示例**：

| 主动 | 被动 |
|------|------|
| We **made** him **captain**. | He **was made captain**. |
| They **elected** her **president**. | She **was elected president**. |
| I **consider** him **honest**. | He **is considered honest**. |
| We **found** the test **difficult**. | The test **was found difficult**. |
| They **named** the baby **Tom**. | The baby **was named Tom**. |

---

### 2.2.4 带情态动词的被动语态

**结构**：`情态动词 + be + 过去分词`

| 情态动词 | 主动 | 被动 |
|----------|------|------|
| can | You can solve the problem. | The problem **can be solved**. |
| must | You must finish the work. | The work **must be finished**. |
| should | You should water the plants. | The plants **should be watered**. |
| might | The storm might damage the crops. | The crops **might be damaged**. |
| could | The news could affect the market. | The market **could be affected**. |
| have to | You have to do it. | It **has to be done**. |
| be going to | They are going to launch it. | It **is going to be launched**. |

---

### 2.2.5 短语动词的被动语态

**定义**：由“动词 + 介词/副词”构成的短语动词（如 look after, take care of, laugh at, call off, put off），在被动语态中要保持短语的**完整性**（介词/副词不能丢掉）。

**结构**：`be + 过去分词 + 介词/副词`

| 短语动词 | 主动 | 被动 |
|----------|------|------|
| look after | She looks after the children. | The children **are looked after** (by her). |
| laugh at | Everyone laughed at him. | He **was laughed at** (by everyone). |
| call off | They called off the meeting. | The meeting **was called off**. |
| put off | We put off the game. | The game **was put off**. |
| take care of | She takes care of the patient. | The patient **is taken care of**. |
| turn down | He turned down the offer. | The offer **was turned down**. |

> **注意**：介词/副词不能省略。例如：`The children are looked after.` 不能说 `The children are looked.`

---

### 2.2.6 被动语态的适用场景

| 场景 | 原因 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| 执行者不明或未知 | 不知道是谁做的 | My wallet **was stolen**.（不知道谁偷的） |
| 执行者不重要 | 关注动作本身而非谁做的 | The bridge **was built** in 1990.（谁建的不重要） |
| 故意省略执行者 | 避免指出责任人 / 委婉表达 | A mistake **was made**.（不说谁犯的错） |
| 客观/正式文体 | 科技论文、新闻报道等 | The experiment **was conducted** three times. |
| 强调承受者 | 承受者比执行者更重要 | The president **was awarded** the Nobel Prize. |

---

### 2.2.7 主动语态与被动语态的选择

| 语态 | 优势 | 适用场景 |
|------|------|----------|
| 主动 | 直接、有力、简洁 | 日常口语、议论文、描述自然动作 |
| 被动 | 客观、正式、强调承受者 | 学术写作、科技论文、新闻报道 |

**雅思写作建议**：
- **Task 1（图表题）**：被动语态非常常见（描述过程图时几乎全程使用被动）
  - 例：The coffee beans **are harvested** → **are roasted** → **are ground**.
- **Task 2（议论文）**：以主动为主，但适当使用被动可以增加句式多样性
  - 主动：Governments should invest more in public transport.
  - 被动：More investment **should be made** in public transport.

## 语态速查表

### 被动语态各时态构成速查

| 时态 | 构成 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| 一般现在时 | am/is/are + done | It **is done**. |
| 一般过去时 | was/were + done | It **was done**. |
| 一般将来时 | will be + done | It **will be done**. |
| 现在进行时 | am/is/are being + done | It **is being done**. |
| 过去进行时 | was/were being + done | It **was being done**. |
| 现在完成时 | have/has been + done | It **has been done**. |
| 过去完成时 | had been + done | It **had been done**. |
| 情态动词 | 情态动词 + be + done | It **must be done**. |

### 主动 → 被动转换步骤
- 步骤1：把原句的宾语（动作承受者）变成新句子的主语
- 步骤2：把原句的谓语动词变成 "be + 过去分词"（be 的时态与原句谓语一致）
- 步骤3：原句的主语（动作执行者）放到 by 后面（可省略）
- 步骤4：其他成分（状语等）保持不变

**示例**：
> 主动：Shakespeare **wrote** *Hamlet* in the 17th century.
> 被动：*Hamlet* **was written** by Shakespeare in the 17th century.

### 不能用于被动语态的动词

| 动词类型 | 原因 | 示例 |
|----------|------|------|
| 不及物动词 | 无宾语 | happen, occur, exist, die, arrive, sleep |
| 系动词 | 表示状态 | be, become, seem, appear, look, feel |
| 某些表示"拥有/属于"的及物动词 | 语义限制 | have, own, possess, belong to, suit, fit |

**错误示例**：
- ❌ A car accident **was happened** yesterday. → ✅ A car accident **happened** yesterday.
- ❌ A new laptop **is had** by me. → ✅ I **have** a new laptop.

1. **写作 Task 1（流程图）**：
   - 全程使用**被动语态**描述流程
   - 例：First, the raw materials **are collected**. Then, they **are transported** to the factory.

2. **写作 Task 1（数据图）**：
   - 主动与被动交替使用，避免单调

3. **写作 Task 2**：

   - 注意短语动词在被动中的完整性（介词不能丢）

5. **口语部分**：
   - 以主动语态为主
   - Part 3 讨论社会话题时适当使用被动：`More parks should be built in cities.`

# 3 倒装

**定义**：倒装是指为了语法结构或修辞需要而**改变正常语序**的句式。英语的基本语序是“**主语 + 谓语**”，倒装则将谓语或谓语的一部分移到主语之前。

**倒装的分类总览**：

| 类型 | 结构 | 特点 |
|------|------|------|
| 正常语序 | 主语 + 谓语 + 宾语/补语 | He is a student. |
| 完全倒装 | 谓语（动词） + 主语 | Here **comes** the bus. |
| 部分倒装 | 助动词/情态动词 + 主语 + 谓语（实义动词） | Never **have I** seen such a thing. |
| 形式倒装 | 语法需求（状语从句等），主谓语序不变 | The more you read, the more you learn. |

> **核心记忆**：
> - **完全倒装**：整个谓语动词移到主语前
> - **部分倒装**：只将助动词/情态动词移到主语前，实义动词留在后面
> - **形式倒装**：主谓语序不变，只是句子结构特殊

---

## 3.1 完全倒装

**定义**：将**整个谓语动词**放在主语之前。通常用于以方位副词、地点介词短语或表语开头的句子中，用以强调或平衡句式。

**结构公式**：
> **状语/表语 + 谓语动词 + 主语**

> **注意**：完全倒装中的谓语动词一般用**不及物动词**（如 come, go, stand, lie, live, be, exist 等）。

### 3.1.1 副词/介词短语在句首的倒装

当表示**方位、地点**的副词（如 here, there, now, then）或介词短语（如 on the wall, in the room）置于句首，且主语为**名词**时，需用完全倒装。

**常见副词**：here, there, now, then, out, in, up, down, away, off, back, over

| 结构 | 正常语序 | 完全倒装 |
|------|----------|----------|
| Here/There + 谓语 + 主语 | The bus comes **here**. | **Here comes the bus**. |
| 方位副词 + 谓语 + 主语 | A dog **runs out**. | **Out runs a dog**. |
| 介词短语 + 谓语 + 主语 | A tree **stands in front of the house**. | **In front of the house stands a tree**. |

**详细示例**：

| 类型 | 示例 | 说明 |
|------|------|------|
| here/there | **Here comes** the bus. | 公交车来了 |
| there | **There goes** the bell. | 铃声响了 |
| then | **Then came** the hour we had been waiting for. | 我们期待的时刻终于到了 |
| now | **Now comes** your turn. | 现在轮到你了 |
| in | **In rushed** the children. | 孩子们冲了进来 |
| out | **Out flew** the bird. | 鸟儿飞了出去 |
| up | **Up went** the arrow into the air. | 箭射向空中 |
| down | **Down fell** the rain. | 雨落了下来 |
| away | **Away ran** the thief. | 小偷逃跑了 |
| off | **Off jumped** the cat. | 猫跳开了 |

**介词短语示例**：

| 介词短语 | 示例 | 正常语序 |
|----------|------|----------|
| on the wall | **On the wall hangs** a painting. | A painting hangs on the wall. |
| in the corner | **In the corner stands** a lamp. | A lamp stands in the corner. |
| under the tree | **Under the tree sat** an old man. | An old man sat under the tree. |
| at the table | **At the table was** a cup of coffee. | A cup of coffee was at the table. |
| between them | **Between them sat** a little girl. | A little girl sat between them. |
| on the floor | **On the floor lay** a cat. | A cat lay on the floor. |

> **注意**：如果主语是**代词**，则不能倒装，只能保持正常语序。
> - ✅ **Here comes** the bus. （bus 是名词 → 倒装）
> - ❌ **Here comes** it.
> - ✅ **Here it comes**. （it 是代词 → 不倒装）

### 3.1.2 主语补语（表语）的倒装

当表语（主语补足语）被置于句首以**强调**时，需将主语和谓语完全倒装。多见于文学或修辞性较强的句子中。

**表语的类型**：形容词、分词、介词短语。

| 表语类型 | 结构 | 示例 |
|----------|------|------|
| 形容词 | 形容词 + be + 主语 | **Happy are** those who are contented. |
| 过去分词 | 过去分词 + be + 主语 | **Gone are** the days when we were young. |
| 现在分词 | 现在分词 + be + 主语 | **Standing in the corner is** a lamp. |
| 介词短语 | 介词短语 + be + 主语 | **Among the guests was** a famous writer. |

**详细示例**：

| 正常语序 | 倒装结构（表语提前） |
|----------|----------------------|
| The days when we were young **are gone**. | **Gone are** the days when we were young. |
| Those who are contented **are happy**. | **Happy are** those who are contented. |
| A lamp **is standing** in the corner. | **Standing in the corner is** a lamp. |
| A famous writer **was among** the guests. | **Among the guests was** a famous writer. |
| The most important thing **is** to take action. | **Most important is** to take action. |
| A shocking sight **was** before them. | **Before them was** a shocking sight. |

**典型句型**：
- **Such is** life. / **Such are** the facts. （这就是生活/这就是事实）
- **Blessed are** the pure in heart. （清心的人有福了——圣经句式）

---

## 3.2 部分倒装

**定义**：只将**助动词、情态动词或 be 动词**提到主语前面，实义动词（主动词）仍保留在原位。

**结构公式**：
> **否定词/only/... + 助动词/情态动词 + 主语 + 实义动词 + ...**

**如果没有助动词，需添加 do/does/did 进行倒装**：
- 正常：I **never** **like** spicy food.
- 倒装：Never **do I like** spicy food. （添加 did，like 恢复原形）

### 3.2.1 句首有否定意义词时的倒装

当**否定词或半否定词**置于句首以强调时，句子需部分倒装。

**常见否定词**：

| 否定词 | 含义 | 示例 |
|--------|------|------|
| never | 从未 | Never have I seen such a sight. |
| seldom | 很少 | Seldom does he go out. |
| rarely | 很少 | Rarely did she complain. |
| hardly | 几乎不 | Hardly had I arrived when it started to rain. |
| scarcely | 几乎不 | Scarcely had he entered when the phone rang. |
| barely | 几乎不 | Barely had she finished when someone knocked. |
| little | 几乎没有 | Little did he know what was coming. |
| nor / neither | 也不 | He didn't come. Nor did she. |
| not | 不 | Not a word did he say. |
| not only | 不仅 | Not only did he pass, but he got an A. |
| no sooner | 一...就... | No sooner had I left than it started to rain. |
| nowhere | 无处 | Nowhere could I find a seat. |
| at no time | 任何时候都不 | At no time did I agree to this. |
| by no means | 绝不 | By no means is it acceptable. |
| in no way | 绝不 | In no way can we allow this. |
| under no circumstances | 绝不 | Under no circumstances should you give up. |
| on no account | 绝不 | On no account must this door be opened. |

**重要结构**（雅思高频）：

| 句型 | 结构 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| Hardly...when... | Hardly + had + 主语 + 过去分词 + when... | **Hardly had I arrived** when the meeting started. |
| Scarcely...when... | Scarcely + had + 主语 + 过去分词 + when... | **Scarcely had she entered** when the phone rang. |
| No sooner...than... | No sooner + had + 主语 + 过去分词 + than... | **No sooner had I left** than it began to rain. |
| Not only...but (also)... | Not only + 助动词 + 主语 + 动词 + but also... | **Not only did he fail**, but he also lost his confidence. |

> **注意**：Hardly/Scarcely/No sooner 句型中，主句用**过去完成时**，从句用**一般过去时**。

### 3.2.2 句首有“only”时的倒装

当 **only + 状语**（副词、介词短语或从句）置于句首时，主句需部分倒装。

**结构公式**：
> **Only + 状语 + 助动词 + 主语 + 实义动词**

| only 引导的状语类型 | 示例 |
|---------------------|------|
| only + 副词 | Only **then** **did I realize** my mistake. |
| only + 介词短语 | Only **by practicing** **can you improve** your English. |
| only + 从句 | Only **when you finish** **will you understand** the importance. |

**更多示例**：

| 结构 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| only then | Only then **did he understand** the truth. |
| only in this way | Only in this way **can we succeed**. |
| only by doing | Only by studying hard **will you pass** the exam. |
| only after | Only after I left **did I realize** how much I loved her. |
| only when | Only when you calm down **can you think** clearly. |
| only once | Only once **have I seen** such a beautiful sunset. |

> **注意**：如果 only 修饰**主语**（即 only + 名词作主语），则不需要倒装。
> - ✅ **Only you** can solve this problem. （only 修饰主语，不倒装）
> - ✅ Only then **did I** realize the truth. （only 修饰状语，倒装）

### 3.2.3 if...should... 构成虚拟语气的倒装

在虚拟条件句中，如果省略 **if**，可以将 **should, were, had** 提到主语前面，构成部分倒装。

**结构公式**：
> **Should/Were/Had + 主语 + 动词原形/过去分词 + ...**

| 原虚拟条件句（if） | 倒装结构（省略 if） |
|--------------------|---------------------|
| If it **should rain** tomorrow, we would cancel the trip. | **Should it rain** tomorrow, we would cancel the trip. |
| If I **were** you, I would go. | **Were I you**, I would go. |
| If he **had come** earlier, he would have seen her. | **Had he come** earlier, he would have seen her. |
| If it **were not** for your help, I would fail. | **Were it not** for your help, I would fail. |
| If it **had not been** for your help, I would have failed. | **Had it not been** for your help, I would have failed. |

### 3.2.4 固定句型中的倒装

以下固定句型中，句子需使用部分倒装。

| 句型 | 结构 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| So + adj/adv + that... | So + adj/adv + 助动词 + 主语 + that... | **So fast did he run** that no one could catch him. |
| Such + be + that... | Such + be + 主语 + that... | **Such is the power** of the Internet that it connects the world. |
| Not until... | Not until + 状语 + 助动词 + 主语 + 动词... | **Not until midnight did he** return home. |
| Neither/Nor...（表示“也不”） | Neither/Nor + 助动词 + 主语 | He doesn't smoke. **Nor does he** drink. |
| So...（表示“也是”） | So + 助动词 + 主语 | She can sing. **So can I**. |
| Adj/adv + as/though... | 形容词/副词 + as/though + 主语 + 动词 | **Tired as he was**, he kept working. |

**详细示例**：

| 句型 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| so...that | **So loud did he shout** that everyone heard him. |
| such...that | **Such was the noise** that I couldn't sleep. |
| not until | **Not until the bell rang did he** stop writing. |
| nor | He didn't come. **Nor did she** call. |
| so（肯定） | I love coffee. **So does my** wife. |
| neither（否定） | I can't swim. **Neither can my** brother. |

**So / Neither / Nor 的简短回答**：

| 前句肯定 | 后句肯定 | 后句否定 |
|----------|----------|----------|
| I like coffee. | **So do I**. | - |
| I don't like tea. | - | **Neither do I** / **Nor do I**. |

## 3.3 形式倒装

**定义**：这类句型虽然改变了正常的语序或结构，但**谓语动词并没有真正提前**（主谓语序保持不变）。“形式倒装”更多是指某些固定句型**打破了常规的主谓宾排列**，以满足语法或修辞的需要，但核心的倒装特征（完全倒装/部分倒装）并不明显。

> **注意**：部分语法体系将这类结构归为“特殊句式”而非严格意义上的倒装。

### 3.3.1 感叹句中的倒装

感叹句中，**what** 或 **how** 引导的感叹结构置于句首，但主谓语序**一般不变**（主语仍在谓语前）。

**结构**：

| 结构 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| What + a/an + adj + 可数名词单数 + 主语 + 谓语！ | **What a beautiful day it is**! |
| What + adj + 可数名词复数 + 主语 + 谓语！ | **What beautiful flowers they are**! |
| What + adj + 不可数名词 + 主语 + 谓语！ | **What lovely weather it is**! |
| How + adj/adv + 主语 + 谓语！ | **How beautiful she is**! |
| How + 主语 + 谓语！ | **How time flies**! |

**感叹句与倒装句的区别**：
- 正常感叹句：**What a lovely day it is**.（主谓顺序：it is，没有倒装）
- 完全倒装：**Here comes the bus**.（谓语 comes 在主语 the bus 之前）

### 3.3.2 比较级句型中的倒装（the more..., the more...）

在 **the + 比较级，the + 比较级** 句型中，主谓语序通常保持不变，但因该结构极其特殊（从句在前，主句在后），故常被视为“形式倒装”。

**结构公式**：
> **The + 比较级 + 主语 + 谓语，the + 比较级 + 主语 + 谓语**

**示例**：

| 结构 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| The more..., the more... | **The more** you practice， **the better** you will become. |
| The harder..., the greater... | **The harder** he worked， **the greater** progress he made. |
| The longer..., the less... | **The longer** I waited， **the less** patient I became. |
| The sooner..., the better... | **The sooner** you start， **the better** your result will be. |

> **注意**：谓语动词一般在主语之后（如 you practice, you will become），没有提前。但因从句倒置在主句之前，结构特殊。

### 3.3.3 however、whatever 引导让步状语从句时的倒装

**however** 和 **whatever** 引导让步状语从句时，可以将**形容词或名词**提前，但主谓语序**保持不变**。

**结构**：

| 引导词 | 结构 | 示例 |
|--------|------|------|
| however | However + adj/adv + 主语 + 谓语 | **However hard he tried**， he failed. |
| whatever | Whatever + 名词 + 主语 + 谓语 | **Whatever difficulties we face**， we will overcome them. |
| whenever | Whenever + 主语 + 谓语 | Whenever you come， you are welcome. |
| wherever | Wherever + 主语 + 谓语 | Wherever he goes， he is happy. |

**更多示例**：

| 结构 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| However + adj | **However rich you are**， you can't buy happiness. |
| However + adv | **However fast you run**， you can't catch him. |
| Whatever + n | **Whatever decision he makes**， I will support him. |
| No matter + 疑问词 | **No matter what you say**， I won't believe you. |

> **注意**：这类结构中，形容词/副词被提前了，但主语和谓语动词的顺序仍然是“主语 + 谓语”，并非典型的倒装。

### 3.3.4 as、though 引导让步状语从句时的倒装

当 **as** 或 **though** 引导让步状语从句时，必须将**形容词、名词、副词或动词原形**提到句首，构成形式倒装。

**结构公式**：
> **形容词/名词/副词/动词 + as/though + 主语 + 谓语 + ...**

| 提前成分 | 结构 | 示例 |
|----------|------|------|
| 形容词 | 形容词 + as/though + 主语 + be | **Tired as he was**， he kept working. |
| 名词 | 名词（无冠词）+ as/though + 主语 + be | **Child as he is**， he knows a lot. |
| 副词 | 副词 + as/though + 主语 + 动词 | **Hard as he tried**， he failed. |
| 动词原形 | 动词原形 + as/though + 主语 + 情态动词 | **Try as he might**， he couldn't solve it. |

**详细示例**：

| 类型 | 示例 | 正常语序 |
|------|------|----------|
| 形容词 | **Poor as he was**， he was generous. | Though he was poor， he was generous. |
| 名词（无冠词） | **Child as he is**， he knows a lot. | Though he is a child， he knows a lot. |
| 副词 | **Fast as he ran**， he couldn't catch up. | Though he ran fast， he couldn't catch up. |
| 动词原形 | **Try as she might**， she couldn't open the door. | Though she might try， she couldn't open the door. |
| much as | **Much as I like you**， I cannot marry you. | Though I like you very much， I cannot marry you. |

> **重要提示**：
> - 如果提前的名词是**可数名词单数**，必须**省略冠词 a/an**。
>   - ✅ **Child as he is**...
>   - ❌ **A child as he is**...
> - **though** 可以替代 **as** 用于此类倒装，但 **although** 不能用于倒装结构。
> - 在非正式文体中，现代英语更倾向用 **although/though** 的正常语序。

## 常见倒装结构对比

| 结构 | 倒装类型 | 示例 |
|------|----------|------|
| Here/There | 完全倒装 | Here comes the bus. |
| 否定词 Never | 部分倒装 | Never have I seen it. |
| Only then | 部分倒装 | Only then did I realize. |
| So fast that | 部分倒装 | So fast did he run that... |
| Were I you | 部分倒装 | Were I you, I would go. |
| Child as he is | 形式倒装 | Child as he is, he knows a lot. |
| The more... | 形式倒装 | The more you practice, the better you get. |

---

## 雅思考试中的倒装句应用

1. **写作 Task 2（议论文）**：
   - 适当使用倒装句可提升**语法多样性**和**句式复杂度**
   - 推荐使用：**否定词倒装**、**only 倒装**、**so...that 倒装**
   - 示例：`Not only does social media connect people, but it also spreads information quickly.`
   - 示例：`Only by taking immediate action can we solve this problem.`

2. **阅读部分**：
   - 识别倒装结构有助于准确理解句子含义
   - 发现助动词在主语前 → 很可能是倒装 → 还原正常语序理解

3. **口语部分**：
   - 避免过度使用倒装，显得不自然
   - 可用简单倒装：`So do I.` / `Neither do I.`（简短回应）

4. **常见错误**：
   - ❌ Only then I realized my mistake. → ✅ Only then **did I realize** my mistake.
   - ❌ Never I have seen such a thing. → ✅ Never **have I seen** such a thing.
   - ❌ So fast he ran that he won. → ✅ So fast **did he run** that he won.
   - ❌ Here comes he. → ✅ Here he comes.（主语为代词时不倒装）

5. **高分句型**（可记忆并套用）：
   - **Not only...but also...**：Not only does exercise improve physical health, but it also enhances mental well-being.
   - **Only by...can...**：Only by working together can we overcome this challenge.
   - **So + adj + be + 主语 + that...**：So profound is the impact of technology that it has reshaped our lives.
   - **Had + 主语 + 过去分词...**：Had I not prepared thoroughly, I would have failed the exam.

# 4 强调

**定义**：强调是为了突出句子中的**特定信息**（某个词、短语或整个句子）而采用的修辞手段，使该信息更加醒目、有力。

**强调手段分类总览**：

| 类别 | 手段 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| 口气（语音） | 重读、语调 | I **LOVE** this movie. |
| 书面格式 | 粗体、斜体、大写 | **Important**: Please read carefully. |
| 词汇强调 | 添加强调词、助动词 | I **do** like it. / It's **very** good. |
| 句式强调 | 倒装、感叹句、强调句型 | It was **Tom** that I saw. |

> **核心记忆**：强调的手段多种多样，从日常口语中的重读到书面语中的特殊句式，目的是让关键信息“跳出来”。

---

## 4.1 口气强调（口语中的强调）

**定义**：通过**发音方式**（重读、语气、语调）来强调某个词或信息，多用于口语表达。

### 4.1.1 重读单词

**定义**：在句子中对某个单词进行**加重读音**，使其听起来比周围的词更响亮、更有力。这是口语中最直接、最简单的强调方式。

**规则**：通常加重读**实义词**（名词、动词、形容词、副词），而非语法词（介词、冠词、连词）。

| 原句（中性） | 强调句（重读） | 强调内容 |
|--------------|----------------|----------|
| I **love** this movie. | I **LOVE** this movie. | 强调“喜爱”的程度 |
| **You** should go. | **YOU** should go. | 强调“你”而不是别人 |
| That is **impossible**. | That is **IMPOSSI BLE**. | 强调“不可能”的程度 |
| She **never** lies. | She **NEVER** lies. | 强调“从不” |

### 4.1.2 用叹词强调

**定义**：通过添加**叹词**（如 oh, wow, ah, ouch, hey, well）来增强情感色彩或引起注意。

**常见叹词及其强调效果**：

| 叹词 | 强调效果 | 示例 |
|------|----------|------|
| Wow | 惊讶/赞叹 | **Wow**! That's amazing! |
| Oh | 惊讶/理解 | **Oh**! Now I understand. |
| Ah | 满足/理解/惊讶 | **Ah**! That explains everything. |
| Hey | 引起注意/警告 | **Hey**! Look out! |
| Well | 犹豫/引出话题 | **Well**, as I was saying... |
| Ouch | 疼痛 | **Ouch**! That hurts! |
| My God | 强烈震惊 | **My God**! Is that true? |

### 4.1.3 使用特定语音/语调/语气强调

**定义**：通过改变**音高、音长、音强**或使用**特殊语气**（夸张、讽刺、郑重等）来强调信息。

| 手段 | 说明 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| 拖长音 | 将某个词的发音拉长 | That was a **loooooong** day. |
| 提高音调 | 在关键词上突然升高音调 | You did **what**? |
| 降低音调 | 放慢速度、降低音调以制造庄重感 | I **solemnly swear**... |
| 停顿 | 在关键词前后稍作停顿 | The answer is... **no**. |
| 夸张语气 | 夸大事实以增强效果 | I've told you a **million** times. |

---

## 4.2 书面格式强调

**定义**：在**书面语**中通过改变**文字格式**（字体、大小写、标点）来突出信息。

### 4.2.1 粗体

**定义**：使用**加粗字体**来强调关键词或短语。常见于教材、说明书、网页、邮件等。

**使用场景**：

| 场景 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| 标题/小标题 | **Chapter 1**: Introduction |
| 关键词 | **Note**: The test begins at 9 am. |
| 警告/提示 | **Warning**: Do not touch the wire. |
| 重要信息 | Please **confirm** your attendance. |

> **雅思提示**：在雅思写作**不宜**使用粗体（考试要求用正常字体）；阅读文章中粗体常帮助定位信息。

### 4.2.2 斜体

**定义**：使用**倾斜字体**来强调、表示书名、外来词或引用的词语。

**使用场景**：

| 场景 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| 强调（较轻） | This is **the most important** factor. |
| 书名/作品名 | I read **Pride and Prejudice**. |
| 外来词 | That's a **deja vu** moment. |
| 引用的词语 | The word **happiness** is hard to define. |
| 语气/思考 | I **really** don't think so. |

> **雅思提示**：雅思机考/笔考中不使用斜体（考试格式限制）。

### 4.2.3 下划线

**定义**：在文字下方画线以强调。常见于手写文本、笔记和部分正式文件中。

**使用场景**：

| 场景 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| 手写笔记 | The meeting is _tomorrow_. |
| 标题 | _Introduction_ to English Grammar |
| 重点 | Please _remember_ to sign the form. |

> **雅思提示**：雅思考试答题纸中**不建议**使用下划线（可正常书写，下划线不是标准格式）。

### 4.2.4 全部大写

**定义**：将整个单词或整个句子使用**大写字母**书写，以表示强烈情感或紧急程度。

> **注意**：全部大写在网络语言中常被视为“喊叫”，应谨慎使用。

**使用场景**：

| 场景 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| 强烈警告 | **DO NOT ENTER**! |
| 极度愤怒/兴奋 | I am **SO** angry! / That's **AMAZING**! |
| 强调单个词 | This is **VERY** important. |
| 头版标题 | **WAR DECLARED**! |

> **雅思提示**：雅思写作**严禁**全部大写（会被视为格式错误，影响可读性）。

### 4.2.5 标点符号

**定义**：通过使用特定的标点符号（感叹号、问号、破折号、省略号等）来强调情感或语气。

| 标点符号 | 强调效果 | 示例 |
|----------|----------|------|
| 感叹号 (!) | 强烈情感、命令、惊讶 | Stop! / What a wonderful day! |
| 问号 (?) | 质疑、反问 | You did **what**? |
| 破折号 (—) | 停顿、强调后续内容 | He has one goal — to win. |
| 省略号 (...) | 悬念、犹豫、未尽之意 | I'm not sure... maybe... |
| 引号 (" ") | 讽刺、特殊含义 | His "help" made things worse. |
| 问号+感叹号 (!?) | 震惊+质疑 | You did **what**!? |

> **雅思提示**：雅思写作中感叹号**不宜使用**（学术写作中感叹号不正式），破折号和省略号也要控制使用频率。

## 4.3 词汇强调

**定义**：通过添加**特定的单词**（强调副词、助动词、重复词等）来增强语气。

### 4.3.1 添加强调词

**定义**：在形容词、副词或动词前添加**强调性词语**（如 very, really, so, such, quite, absolutely, completely, definitely, literally 等）。

**常见强调词分类**：

| 类别 | 强调词 | 示例 |
|------|--------|------|
| 程度强调 | very, so, too, extremely, incredibly | It's **very** cold. / She's **so** kind. |
| 肯定强调 | really, absolutely, definitely, certainly | I **really** like it. / It's **absolutely** perfect. |
| 强调事实 | indeed, actually, in fact | It is **indeed** a great honor. |
| 强调唯一 | only, just, simply | It's **simply** the best. / I **just** love it. |
| 强调整体 | quite, rather, pretty | That's **quite** impressive. |

**典型结构**：

| 结构 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| so + adj | You are **so** kind. |
| such + (a/an) + (adj) + n | It was **such** a beautiful day. |
| very + adj/adv | He runs **very** fast. |
| really + v/adj | I **really** appreciate your help. |
| absolutely + adj | It's **absolutely** wonderful. |
| too + adj/adv | It's **too** expensive. |

### 4.3.2 重复单词

**定义**：通过**重复**同一个词或短语来增强语气，表示程度深、数量多或强烈情感。

**重复类型**：

| 类型 | 结构 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| 形容词重复 | adj + adj + 名词 | It's a **long, long** journey. |
| 副词重复 | adv + adv + 动词 | He ran **far, far** away. |
| 名词重复 | 名词 + after/and + 名词 | He worked **day after day**. / They talked **hour after hour**. |
| 动词重复 | 动词 + and + 动词 | She **cried and cried**. / They **waited and waited**. |
| 介词短语重复 | again and again / over and over | I've told you **again and again**. |

**更多示例**：

| 结构 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| 强调程度 | It's a **very, very** important issue. |
| 强调时间 | I waited for **hours and hours**. |
| 强调距离 | They walked **miles and miles**. |
| 强调情感 | I'm **so, so** sorry. |
| 强调数量 | There were **hundreds and hundreds** of people. |

### 4.3.3 使用助动词

**定义**：在肯定句中，添加助动词 **do, does, did** 放在实义动词前，以加强语气、强调事实或表达强烈肯定。

**结构公式**：
> **do/does/did + 动词原形**

**示例**：

| 中性句（无强调） | 强调句（加助动词） |
|------------------|---------------------|
| I like coffee. | I **do** like coffee.（我真的喜欢咖啡） |
| She looks beautiful. | She **does** look beautiful.（她确实漂亮） |
| He came yesterday. | He **did** come yesterday.（他昨天确实来了） |
| We need your help. | We **do** need your help.（我们确实需要你的帮助） |
| That makes sense. | That **does** make sense.（那确实有道理） |

> **注意**：添加助动词后，原来的实义动词必须恢复为**原形**。
> - 不能说：I do **likes** coffee.
> - 正确：I do **like** coffee.

**在回应中表示强调**：

| 对话 | 强调回应 |
|------|----------|
| You don't like him, do you? | I **do** like him!（我确实喜欢他！） |
| You didn't finish? | I **did** finish!（我确实完成了！） |

### 4.3.4 强调代词

**定义**：使用**反身代词**（如 myself, yourself, himself, herself, itself, ourselves, yourselves, themselves）放在被强调的名词/代词后面，表示“**本人/亲自**”。

**结构公式**：
> **主语/宾语 + 反身代词**

**示例**：

| 类型 | 示例 | 含义 |
|------|------|------|
| 强调主语 | I **myself** will do it. | 我亲自做 |
| 强调主语 | The president **himself** attended. | 总统本人出席了 |
| 强调主语 | She **herself** told me. | 她亲口告诉我的 |
| 强调宾语 | I met the CEO **himself**. | 我见到了CEO本人 |
| 强调宾语 | I want to speak to Tom **himself**. | 我要跟汤姆本人谈 |

**与反身代词其他用法的区别**：

| 用法 | 示例 | 区别 |
|------|------|------|
| 强调代词 | I **myself** fixed the car. | 去掉 myself 句子仍完整（I fixed the car） |
| 反身代词（动作反射） | I hurt **myself**. | 去掉 myself 句子不完整（I hurt 缺少宾语） |

## 4.4 句式强调

**定义**：通过改变**句子结构**（倒装、感叹句、强调句型）来突出某个信息。

### 4.4.1 倒装

**定义**：通过将**否定词、only、so/such** 等置于句首并采用部分倒装来强调。

> **注**：倒装的详细内容已在第3章（倒装）中完整介绍，此处仅列出强调功能相关的结构。

**常见强调性倒装结构**：

| 结构 | 示例 | 强调内容 |
|------|------|----------|
| 否定词 + 倒装 | Never **have I seen** such beauty. | “从未”见过如此美景 |
| Only + 状语 + 倒装 | Only then **did I realize** my mistake. | “只有那时”才意识到 |
| So + adj + 倒装 + that | So fast **did he run** that no one could catch him. | 跑得“如此之快” |
| Not only...but also... | Not only **did he pass**, but he got an A. | “不仅”通过了 |

### 4.4.2 感叹句

**定义**：用 **What** 或 **How** 引导的感叹句来强调形容词、名词或整个事件的情感色彩。

**结构公式**：

| 结构 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| What + a/an + adj + 可数单数 + 主谓 | **What a beautiful day it is**! |
| What + adj + 可数复数 + 主谓 | **What beautiful flowers they are**! |
| What + adj + 不可数名词 + 主谓 | **What lovely weather it is**! |
| How + adj/adv + 主谓 | **How beautiful she is**! |
| How + 主谓 | **How time flies**! |

> **注**：感叹句的详细内容已在第1章（句子分类）中介绍。

### 4.4.3 it + be + that 强调句

**定义**：强调句型 **It is/was + 被强调部分 + that/who + 其他**，是最重要的句式强调手段。可以强调除谓语动词以外的任何句子成分（主语、宾语、状语等）。

**结构公式**：
> **It + be动词（is/was） + 被强调部分 + that/who + 句子剩余部分**

**转换规则**：

| 原句 | It was...that... 强调句 |
|------|-------------------------|
| Tom met Mary in the park yesterday. | → 强调主语：**It was Tom** that met Mary in the park yesterday. |
| | → 强调宾语：**It was Mary** that Tom met in the park yesterday. |
| | → 强调地点状语：**It was in the park** that Tom met Mary yesterday. |
| | → 强调时间状语：**It was yesterday** that Tom met Mary in the park. |

**被强调部分的类型**：

| 类型 | 结构 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| 强调主语（人） | It is/was + 人 + who/that | **It was Tom who** called you. |
| 强调主语（物） | It is/was + 物 + that | **It is this book that** changed my life. |
| 强调宾语 | It is/was + 宾语 + that | **It was this car that** I bought. |
| 强调时间状语 | It is/was + 时间 + that | **It was last night that** I saw him. |
| 强调地点状语 | It is/was + 地点 + that | **It was in London that** they met. |
| 强调方式状语 | It is/was + 方式 + that | **It was with great joy that** he accepted. |
| 强调原因状语 | It is/was + 原因 + that | **It was because of the rain that** we stayed home. |

**时态选择**：

| 原句时态 | 强调句 be 动词 | 示例 |
|----------|----------------|------|
| 现在/将来时 | **is** | It **is** you that **are** wrong. |
| 过去时 | **was** | It **was** yesterday that I **saw** her. |
| 现在完成时 | **is**（强调现在） | It **is** the book that **has influenced** me. |

**that 与 who 的选择**：

| 被强调部分 | 引导词 | 示例 |
|------------|--------|------|
| 人（主语/宾语） | **who** 或 **that** | It was Tom **who/that** helped me. |
| 物/事/其他 | **that**（不可用 who） | It was this book **that** I wanted. |
| 时间/地点/原因/方式 | **that** | It was here **that** we met. |

> **注意**：强调句型可以强调除了谓语动词以外的任何成分。如果要强调谓语动词本身，需要用“助动词 do/does/did + 动词原形”（见 4.3.3）。

**强调句与主语从句的区别**：

| 类型 | 示例 | 特点 |
|------|------|------|
| 强调句 | It was **Tom** that helped me. | 去掉 It was...that 后：**Tom helped me**（完整句子） |
| 主语从句 | It was strange **that** he didn't come. | 去掉 It was...that 后：**strange he didn't come**（不完整） |

**判断方法**：去掉 It is/was...that/who 后，剩下的部分如果是一个**完整句子**，则是强调句；如果不完整，则是主语从句（或其他句型）。

## 雅思考试中的强调句应用

1. **写作 Task 2（议论文）**：
   - **推荐使用**：
     - `do/does/did + 动词原形`：`Governments do need to invest more in education.`
     - `It is/was...that...` 强调句：`It is the government that should take responsibility.`
     - 倒装句：`Not only does this policy save money, but it also protects the environment.`
   - **不推荐使用**：
     - 感叹句（不正式）
     - 全部大写（格式错误）
     - 粗体/斜体/下划线（考试格式不支持）

2. **口语部分**：
   - 自然使用：重读、强调词、`do/does/did` 强调
   - 适当使用：`It is...that...` 强调句
   - 示例：`The thing is, I do believe that...` / `It was my mother who inspired me most.`

3. **阅读部分**：
   - 识别强调句型（`It is...that...`）→ 快速定位作者想要突出的信息
   - 识别倒装句 → 还原正常语序理解

4. **常见错误**：
   - ❌ It was yesterday **when** I saw him. → ✅ It was yesterday **that** I saw him.
   - ❌ It is the book **who** changed my life. → ✅ It is the book **that** changed my life.
   - ❌ I **do likes** coffee. → ✅ I **do like** coffee.
   - ❌ It was Tom that **he** helped me. → ✅ It was Tom that helped me.（不能重复主语）

---

## 强调句型对比（it is...that...）

| 原句 | 强调主语 | 强调宾语 | 强调状语 |
|------|----------|----------|----------|
| John bought a car yesterday. | It was **John** who bought a car yesterday. | It was **a car** that John bought yesterday. | It was **yesterday** that John bought a car. |
| I met her in Paris. | It was **I** who met her in Paris. | It was **her** that I met in Paris. | It was **in Paris** that I met her. |
| The rain caused the delay. | It was **the rain** that caused the delay. | - | - |

**注意**：强调句型可以同时强调多个成分吗？不能。一个强调句只能强调**一个**成分。

# 5 省略

**定义**：为了避免重复、使表达简洁，在句中**省去某些成分**（一个或多个词）的现象。省略必须遵循**不改变句意**且**不引起歧义**的原则。

**省略的分类总览**：

| 类别 | 内容 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| 词法省略 | 冠词、介词、动词不定式、从句引导词的省略 | I went to (the) school. |
| 句法省略 | 简单句、并列句、复杂句中的省略 | He likes coffee, and she (likes) tea. |

> **核心记忆**：能省则省，但要保证意思清楚、语法正确。

---

## 5.1 词法省略

**定义**：省略句子中的**冠词、介词、动词不定式符号 to、从句引导词**等语法功能词。

### 5.1.1 省略冠词

#### “独一无二”

**定义**：当名词表示**独一无二**的事物（如职位、头衔、身份、家庭成员等），尤其是在 be 动词后作表语或用于称呼时，冠词常被省略。

| 情况 | 示例 | 说明 |
|------|------|------|
| 独一无二的职位/头衔 | He was elected **president**. | (the president 的 the 省略) |
| | She became **CEO** at 30. | (the CEO 的 the 省略) |
| | Who is **captain** of the team? | (the captain 的 the 省略) |
| 家庭成员称呼 | **Father** is not at home. | (my father → Father) |
| | **Mom**, can I go out? | (my mom → Mom) |
| 并列结构中避免重复 | a man and (a) woman | 第二个 a 省略 |
| | the teacher and (the) students | 第二个 the 省略 |

> **注意**：只有**独一无二**或**身份明确**时才可省略冠词；如果表示泛指或首次提到，一般保留。

#### 并列关系

**定义**：在两个或多个并列的名词、形容词或短语中，如果冠词重复出现，可以省略后面的冠词。

| 省略前 | 省略后 | 说明 |
|--------|--------|------|
| a man and a woman | a man and woman | 第二个 a 省略 |
| the teacher and the students | the teacher and students | 第二个 the 省略 |
| a red and a blue car | a red and blue car | 第二个 a 省略（共指一辆车） |

> **区分**：`a red and a blue car` 指两辆车（一辆红、一辆蓝）；`a red and blue car` 指一辆车（红蓝相间）。

#### 独立主格

**定义**：在独立主格结构中，冠词有时可以省略，尤其是当名词为抽象名词或表示身份时。

| 结构 | 示例 | 说明 |
|------|------|------|
| 名词 + 形容词/分词 | He entered the room, **hat on (his) head**. | his 省略 |
| | She sat there, **book in hand**. | a book 的 a 省略 |
| 名词 + 介词短语 | He slept, **windows open**. | the windows 的 the 省略 |

---

### 5.1.2 省略介词

#### 时间介词

**定义**：在一些表示**时间**的表达中，介词 **at, on, in** 可以省略，尤其是当时间词与某些动词或特定结构连用时。

| 省略前 | 省略后 | 说明 |
|--------|--------|------|
| We will meet **on** Monday. | We will meet Monday. | on 省略（美式英语常见） |
| He arrived **on** May 5th. | He arrived May 5th. | on 省略 |
| I work **on** every Friday. | I work every Friday. | on 省略 |
| She will come **in** next week. | She will come next week. | in 省略 |
| He came **on** that day. | He came that day. | on 省略 |

> **注意**：这种省略在**美式英语**中更常见；英式英语通常保留介词。

#### 做某事（表示“花费/做...”的结构）

**定义**：在 `spend time (in) doing` 和 `have difficulty (in) doing` 等结构中，介词 **in** 可以省略。

| 结构 | 省略前 | 省略后 |
|------|--------|--------|
| spend time in doing | I spent two hours **in** finishing it. | I spent two hours finishing it. |
| have difficulty in doing | She has difficulty **in** understanding. | She has difficulty understanding. |
| have trouble in doing | He has trouble **in** solving it. | He has trouble solving it. |
| have fun in doing | We had fun **in** playing games. | We had fun playing games. |

> **注意**：`busy (in) doing` 中的 in 也常省略：`She is busy (in) preparing dinner.`

---

### 5.1.3 省略动词不定式

#### 并列关系

**定义**：当两个或多个不定式由并列连词（and, or, but, than）连接时，**第二个及之后的不定式可省略 to**。

| 省略前 | 省略后 | 说明 |
|--------|--------|------|
| I want **to** eat and **to** drink. | I want **to** eat and drink. | 第二个 to 省略 |
| He decided **to** stay or **to** leave. | He decided **to** stay or leave. | 第二个 to 省略 |
| She came **to** help but not **to** criticize. | She came **to** help but not criticize. | 第二个 to 省略 |

> **注意**：如果两个不定式表示**对比**或**强调区别**，to 通常不省略。如：`I came not to praise him but to bury him.`（to 不省略）

#### 裸不定式（感官动词/使役动词后的 to 省略）

**定义**：在**感官动词**（see, hear, watch, notice, feel 等）和**使役动词**（make, let, have 等）后面作宾语补足语时，不定式符号 **to** 必须省略。

| 动词类型 | 结构 | 示例 |
|----------|------|------|
| 感官动词 | see/hear/watch + 宾语 + 动词原形 | I saw him **come** in. |
| 使役动词 | make/let/have + 宾语 + 动词原形 | She made me **cry**. |
| 帮助动词 | help + 宾语 + (to) 动词原形 | He helped me **(to)** clean the room.（to 可省略） |

**示例**：

| 主动（to 省略） | 被动（to 恢复） |
|----------------|-----------------|
| I saw him **enter** the room. | He was seen **to enter** the room. |
| She made me **do** it. | I was made **to do** it. |
| We heard her **sing**. | She was heard **to sing**. |

> **核心规律**：主动句中感官/使役动词后 to 省略；变成被动句时，to 必须恢复。

---

### 5.1.4 省略从句引导词

**定义**：在名词性从句（尤其是宾语从句）和定语从句中，引导词在某些条件下可以省略。

| 从句类型 | 可省略的引导词 | 条件 | 示例 |
|----------|----------------|------|------|
| 宾语从句 | **that** | 非正式文体，不引起歧义 | I think **(that)** he is right. |
| 定语从句 | **that/which/who(m)** | 在从句中作**宾语**时 | The book **(that)** I bought is good. |
| 定语从句 | **where/when/why** | 可与 that 互换时 | That's the reason **(why)** he left. |
| 表语从句 | **that** | 有时省略（非正式） | My idea is **(that)** we should wait. |

**详细示例**：

| 原句（无省略） | 省略后 |
|----------------|--------|
| I believe **that** she is honest. | I believe she is honest. |
| He said **that** he would come. | He said he would come. |
| The book **that** I read is interesting. | The book I read is interesting. |
| The man **whom** you met is my uncle. | The man you met is my uncle. |
| The reason **why** he came is unknown. | The reason he came is unknown. |

> **注意**：以下情况 **that 不能省略**：
> 1. 引导主语从句：**That** he is honest is obvious.（that 不能省）
> 2. 引导同位语从句：The news **that** he won is true.（that 不能省）
> 3. 引导表语从句：My opinion is **that** we should go.（that 可省，但有歧义时不省）
> 4. 从句位于句首：**That** she is beautiful, we all know.（that 不能省）

---

## 5.2 句法省略

**定义**：省略句子中的**主语、谓语、宾语、从句或整个分句**等句法成分。

### 5.2.1 简单句省略

#### 祈使句

**定义**：祈使句省略主语 **you**（有时也省略谓语动词）。

| 省略前 | 省略后 | 说明 |
|--------|--------|------|
| **You** come in. | Come in. | 省略主语 you |
| **You** be quiet. | Be quiet. | 省略主语 you |
| **You** don't do that. | Don't do that. | 省略主语 you |
| **You** let me see. | Let me see. | 省略主语 you |

#### 感叹句

**定义**：感叹句中省略主语和谓语（常省略 it is, they are 等）。

| 省略前 | 省略后 | 说明 |
|--------|--------|------|
| What a beautiful day **it is**! | What a beautiful day! | 省略 it is |
| How fast **he runs**! | How fast! | 省略 he runs |
| What a shame **it is**! | What a shame! | 省略 it is |
| How wonderful **it is**! | How wonderful! | 省略 it is |

#### 口语表达

**定义**：在日常口语中，经常省略句首的 **I, it, that, there** 等，或省略谓语动词。

| 省略前 | 省略后 | 说明 |
|--------|--------|------|
| **I** don't know. | Don't know. | 省略主语 I |
| **It** doesn't matter. | Doesn't matter. | 省略主语 It |
| **I have** no idea. | No idea. | 省略 I have |
| **There is** no problem. | No problem. | 省略 There is |
| **I will** see you later. | See you later. | 省略 I will |
| **I am** sorry. | Sorry. | 省略 I am |
| **Do you** want some? | Want some? | 省略 Do you |

> **注意**：这些省略主要出现在**非正式口语**中，雅思写作中应避免。

---

### 5.2.2 并列句的省略

**定义**：在并列句中，如果后一分句中有与前一分句**相同**的成分，可以省略，以避免重复。

**结构**：`分句1 + 连词 + 分句2（省略相同部分）`

| 省略前 | 省略后 | 说明 |
|--------|--------|------|
| He likes coffee and **he likes** tea. | He likes coffee and tea. | 省略主语和谓语 |
| I went to Paris, and **I went to** London. | I went to Paris, and London. | 省略主语和谓语 |
| She can sing, and **she can** dance. | She can sing, and dance. | 省略主语和情态动词 |
| Tom is tall, and **Tom is** strong. | Tom is tall and strong. | 省略主语和 be 动词 |
| He has read the book, but **he has** not **read** the report. | He has read the book, but not the report. | 省略主语和助动词 |

**省略宾语**：

| 省略前 | 省略后 |
|--------|--------|
| I like coffee, and she likes **coffee** too. | I like coffee, and she does too. |
| He speaks English, and I speak **English** too. | He speaks English, and I do too. |

**省略谓语动词**：

| 省略前 | 省略后 |
|--------|--------|
| Tom plays football, and Jerry **plays** basketball. | Tom plays football, and Jerry basketball. |
| She ordered steak, and he **ordered** fish. | She ordered steak, and he fish. |

**常见模式**：

| 模式 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| 主语 + 谓语 + 宾语1, and 宾语2 | She bought apples, bananas, and oranges. |
| 主语 + 情态动词1, and 动词2 | You can come and go as you please. |
| 主语 + 动词 + 宾语, and 主语 + 助动词 | He likes coffee, and she does too. |

---

### 5.2.3 复杂句的省略

#### 从句中的省略

**定义**：在状语从句和名词性从句中，如果主语与主句主语一致且谓语包含 be 动词，可以省略“**主语 + be**”。

**1. 时间状语从句的省略**

| 原句 | 省略后 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| **When I am** young, I work hard. | **When young**, I work hard. | 省略 I am |
| **While he was** walking, he saw a bird. | **While walking**, he saw a bird. | 省略 he was |
| **After she was** told the news, she cried. | **After being told** the news, she cried. | 省略 she was（保留 being） |

**2. 条件状语从句的省略**

| 原句 | 省略后 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| **If it is** possible, I will come. | **If possible**, I will come. | 省略 it is |
| **If you are** in doubt, ask me. | **If in doubt**, ask me. | 省略 you are |
| **Unless you are** invited, don't come. | **Unless invited**, don't come. | 省略 you are |
| **If it is** necessary, we will help. | **If necessary**, we will help. | 省略 it is |

**3. 让步状语从句的省略**

| 原句 | 省略后 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| **Though he is** young, he knows a lot. | **Though young**, he knows a lot. | 省略 he is |
| **Although it was** cold, he went out. | **Although cold**, he went out. | 省略 it was |
| **Even if I am** tired, I will go. | **Even if tired**, I will go. | 省略 I am |

**4. 比较状语从句的省略**

| 原句 | 省略后 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| He is taller than **I am**. | He is taller than **I**. | 省略 am |
| She runs faster than **he does**. | She runs faster than **he**. | 省略 does |
| I like you more than **I like** him. | I like you more than **him**. | 省略 I like（注意歧义） |

> **歧义提示**：`I like you more than him.` 可以理解为：
> - `I like you more than (I like) him.`（我喜欢你胜过喜欢他）
> - `I like you more than he (likes you).`（我比他更喜欢你）
> 为避免歧义，可补全成分或换用其他表达。

**5. 定语从句中的省略**

| 情况 | 示例 | 说明 |
|------|------|------|
| 关系代词作宾语 | The book **(that)** I bought is good. | that 可省略 |
| 关系代词 + be | The man **(who is)** sitting there is Tom. | who is 可省略 |
| there be 结构中 | This is the best book **(that there is)**. | that there is 可省略 |

**定语从句中省略“关系代词 + be”**：

| 原句 | 省略后 |
|------|--------|
| The man **who is** standing there is Tom. | The man standing there is Tom. |
| The book **that is** written by him is popular. | The book written by him is popular. |
| The girl **who is** beautiful is my sister. | The girl beautiful is my sister. ❌（形容词作定语需前置：The beautiful girl） |

> **注意**：省略 `关系代词 + be` 后，留下的现在分词、过去分词或介词短语可以直接作后置定语，但单个形容词不能后置。

#### 主句中的省略

**定义**：在复杂句中，有时可以省略**整个主句**或主句中的部分成分，尤其是在对话或特定语境中。

**1. 省略主句（常见于问答）**

| 对话 | 省略前 | 省略后 |
|------|--------|--------|
| Q: Why are you late? | **I am late because** the bus was late. | Because the bus was late. |
| Q: What did he say? | **He said that** he would come. | That he would come. |
| Q: Are you coming? | **I will come if** I can. | If I can. |

**2. 省略主句中的谓语**

| 原句 | 省略后 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| He is taller than I **am tall**. | He is taller than I. | 省略 am tall |
| She can run faster than he **can run**. | She can run faster than he. | 省略 can run |

**3. 省略主句中的宾语**

| 原句 | 省略后 | 说明 |
|------|--------|------|
| I believe **that he is right**, and she believes it too. | I believe that he is right, and she does **too**. | 省略宾语从句 |
| He likes coffee, and I like **coffee** too. | He likes coffee, and I do **too**. | 省略宾语 |

## 省略的注意事项和常见错误

### 省略的基本原则

| 原则 | 说明 |
|------|------|
| 不改变句意 | 省略后原句的核心意思必须保持不变 |
| 不引起歧义 | 省略不能导致多种可能的理解 |
| 语法可恢复 | 被省略的部分可以根据语法规则补全 |

### 常见错误

| 错误类型 | 错误示例 | 正确示例 | 说明 |
|----------|----------|----------|------|
| 省略引起歧义 | I like you more than him. | I like you more than I like him. / I like you more than he does. | 补全以避免歧义 |
| 不该省略时省略 | He is taller than me. (比较级中) | He is taller than I (am). | 正式语体中保留主格 |
| 从句主语不一致时省略 | When young, the car hit him. | When he was young, the car hit him. | 从句主语是 he，主句主语是 the car，不一致 |
| 省略后不完整 | She is taller than. | She is taller than me. | than 后面必须有比较对象 |

## 雅思考试中的省略应用

1. **写作 Task 2（议论文）**：
   - **推荐使用**：
     - 定语从句中关系代词作宾语的省略：`The book I read was interesting.`
     - 并列句中的省略：`Some people prefer cities, while others prefer the countryside.`
     - 条件/让步状语从句的省略：`If possible, we should reduce pollution.`
   - **谨慎使用**：
     - `that` 在宾语从句中的省略（可能显得不够正式）
     - 比较级中的省略（确保不引起歧义）
   - **避免使用**：
     - 口语化的省略：`No idea.` / `See you later.`
     - 歧义省略：`I like you more than him.`（建议补全）

2. **阅读部分**：
   - 识别省略结构有助于准确理解句子
   - 遇到 `If possible`，`When young` 等结构，能自动补全被省略的 `it is` / `I am`
   - 在比较结构中，注意判断省略是否引起歧义

3. **口语部分**：
   - 省略使用非常频繁，是自然口语的特征
   - 祈使句、感叹句、口语省略（`No problem`, `My treat` 等）可以大胆使用
   - 注意不要过度省略导致对方听不懂

4. **语法填空/改错题**：
   - 判断省略是否恰当（是否引起歧义、是否符合语法规则）
   - 识别定语从句中关系代词的省略条件
   - 识别 `spend time (in) doing` 中介词 in 的省略

# 6 主谓一致

**定义**：主谓一致是指**主语**和**谓语动词**之间在人称和数上保持一致的关系。英语中，谓语动词的形式必须随着主语的人称和数而变化。

**主谓一致的三大原则总览**：

| 原则 | 核心规则 | 示例 |
|------|----------|------|
| 语法一致 | 主语单数 → 谓语单数；主语复数 → 谓语复数 | He **likes** coffee. / They **like** tea. |
| 意义一致 | 根据主语的**实际意义**（而非语法形式）决定谓语 | The team **is** strong. (整体) / The team **are** arguing. (成员) |
| 就近一致 | 谓语与**最近的主语**保持一致 | Either you or he **is** wrong. / There **is** a pen and two books. |

> **核心记忆**：谓语的形式要么跟着主语的**语法形式**走，要么跟着主语的**实际意义**走，要么跟着**最近的主语**走。

---

## 6.1 语法一致

**定义**：谓语动词的**单复数形式**与其主语的**语法形式**（单数或复数）保持一致。这是主谓一致最基本、最常用的原则。

**核心规则**：
- 主语是**单数** → 谓语用**单数**（动词加 -s/-es，或 be 动词用 is/was）
- 主语是**复数** → 谓语用**复数**（动词用原形，或 be 动词用 are/were）

### 6.1.1 单数主语/复数主语

| 主语类型 | 谓语要求 | 示例 |
|----------|----------|------|
| 可数名词单数 | 单数谓语 | The **student is** studying. / A **car was** parked here. |
| 可数名词复数 | 复数谓语 | The **students are** studying. / **Cars were** parked here. |
| 不可数名词 | 单数谓语 | **Water is** important. / **Information has** been updated. |
| 代词（单数） | 单数谓语 | **He likes** coffee. / **Everything is** fine. |
| 代词（复数） | 复数谓语 | **We are** ready. / **They have** arrived. |
| 数词（单数） | 单数谓语 | **Two is** enough. / **Ten miles is** a long walk. |
| 动词不定式/动名词 | 单数谓语 | **To learn English is** important. / **Running is** fun. |

### 6.1.2 假象主语

**定义**：有些结构看似有多个主语或复杂的主语，但其核心主语仍然是单数或复数，谓语须与**真正的主语**保持一致。

#### 1. 带有 with / together with / along with / as well as / including 的结构

**核心规则**：这些介词短语**不改变**主语的数。谓语只与**前面的主语**保持一致，不受介词短语中名词的影响。

| 结构 | 示例 | 说明 |
|------|------|------|
| with | The teacher **with** his students **is** on the bus. | 谓语与 teacher 一致（单数） |
| together with | Tom, **together with** his friends, **is** coming. | 谓语与 Tom 一致（单数） |
| along with | The captain, **along with** his team, **was** honored. | 谓语与 captain 一致（单数） |
| as well as | Mary, **as well as** her parents, **is** at the party. | 谓语与 Mary 一致（单数） |
| including | Everyone, **including** the teachers, **is** invited. | 谓语与 Everyone 一致（单数） |
| in addition to | The manager, **in addition to** his staff, **was** present. | 谓语与 manager 一致（单数） |

> **对比**：
> - **and**：The teacher **and** his students **are** on the bus.（两个独立主语 → 复数谓语）
> - **with**：The teacher **with** his students **is** on the bus.（伴随状语 → 单数谓语）

#### 2. 由 and 连接的主语

**规则A**：由 **and** 连接的两个或多个单数主语，谓语通常用**复数**。

| 示例 | 说明 |
|------|------|
| Tom **and** Jerry **are** friends. | 两个不同的人 → 复数 |
| Coffee **and** tea **are** served. | 两种物品 → 复数 |
| The teacher **and** the student **have** arrived. | 两个人 → 复数 |

**规则B**：由 **and** 连接的两个名词表示**同一个人、同一事物**或**同一概念**时，谓语用**单数**。

| 示例 | 说明 |
|------|------|
| The singer **and** songwriter **is** famous. | 同一个人（既是歌手又是词曲作者） |
| Bread **and** butter **is** my favorite breakfast. | 同一事物（面包加黄油） |
| Law **and** order **has** been restored. | 同一概念（法治秩序） |
| Fish **and** chips **is** a traditional British dish. | 同一道菜 |
| Time **and** tide **waits** for no man. | 谚语（时光不等人） |

> **如何区分**：如果 and 前面的名词有冠词（a/an/the）或物主代词重复出现，通常表示不同的人/物。
> - The singer **and** the songwriter **are** here.（两个人：歌手和词曲作者）
> - The singer **and** songwriter **is** here.（同一个人）

#### 3. 由 or / nor / either...or / neither...nor 连接的主语

**规则**：谓语与**最近的主语**保持一致（详见 6.3 就近一致）。

| 示例 | 说明 |
|------|------|
| Either you **or** I **am** wrong. | 谓语与 I 一致 |
| Neither the manager **nor** the employees **are** happy. | 谓语与 employees 一致 |
| Tom **or** his brothers **are** coming. | 谓语与 brothers 一致 |

#### 4. every / each / no 修饰的主语

**规则**：由 **every**、**each**、**no** 修饰的多个单数主语，即使由 and 连接，谓语仍用**单数**。

| 示例 | 说明 |
|------|------|
| **Every** boy **and** girl **is** excited. | 每一个男孩和女孩 |
| **Each** man **and** woman **has** a ticket. | 每一位男士和女士 |
| **No** teacher **and** student **was** injured. | 没有一个老师和学生 |

#### 5. many a / more than one 修饰的主语

**规则**：虽然表示复数意义，但谓语用**单数**。

| 结构 | 示例 | 说明 |
|------|------|------|
| many a + 单数名词 | **Many a student has** made this mistake. | 许多学生犯过这个错误 |
| more than one + 单数名词 | **More than one person is** involved. | 不止一个人参与 |

> **注意**：`more + 复数名词 + than one` 则谓语用复数：`More persons than one **are** involved.`

### 6.1.3 不定代词作主语

**定义**：由 some-, any-, no-, every- 构成的复合不定代词（如 someone, anybody, nothing, everything 等）作主语时，谓语用**单数**。

**单数谓语的不定代词**（全部）：

| 类型 | 示例 | 谓语 |
|------|------|------|
| 指人 | someone, somebody, anyone, anybody, no one, nobody, everyone, everybody | 单数 |
| 指物 | something, anything, nothing, everything | 单数 |
| 其他 | each, either, neither, one | 单数 |

**示例**：
- **Everyone is** here.
- **Something has** gone wrong.
- **Nothing was** found.
- **Each of the students has** a book.
- **Neither of the answers is** correct.

**复数谓语的不定代词**（少数）：

| 不定代词 | 谓语 | 示例 |
|----------|------|------|
| both | 复数 | **Both are** good. |
| few / a few | 复数 | **Few have** passed. |
| many | 复数 | **Many have** tried. |
| several | 复数 | **Several were** injured. |

**可单可复的不定代词**（根据意义决定）：

| 不定代词 | 谓语规则 | 示例 |
|----------|----------|------|
| all | 指整体 → 单数；指个体 → 复数 | All **is** lost. (一切都完了) / All **are** here. (所有人都到了) |
| some | 指不可数 → 单数；指可数 → 复数 | Some **is** left. (还剩一些) / Some **are** broken. (有些坏了) |
| none | 正式文体多用单数；口语可用复数 | None **has** arrived. / None **have** arrived. (均可) |
| most | 指不可数 → 单数；指可数 → 复数 | Most **is** gone. / Most **are** here. |

## 6.2 意义一致

**定义**：谓语动词的单复数形式不取决于主语的**语法形式**，而取决于主语的**实际意义**（即说话者想要表达的是整体概念还是个体成员）。

### 6.2.1 同一个主语不同身份

**定义**：同一个主语（尤其是人）在不同的语境中可能被视为单数或复数，取决于说话者强调的方面。

| 主语 | 单数意义（强调整体） | 复数意义（强调成员） |
|------|----------------------|----------------------|
| the family | My family **is** large. (家庭规模大) | My family **are** early risers. (家人起得早) |
| the team | The team **is** the best. (队伍整体) | The team **are** arguing. (队员在争吵) |
| the class | The class **has** 30 students. (班级规模) | The class **are** doing homework. (全班同学) |
| the government | The government **has** decided. (政府整体决策) | The government **are** divided. (官员意见分歧) |

> **说明**：美式英语倾向于将集合名词视为**单数**（用单数谓语）；英式英语可根据语境使用单数或复数。

### 6.2.2 复数名词的单数意义

**定义**：有些名词形式上是**复数**（以 -s 结尾），但意义上表示**单数**概念，谓语用单数。

| 名词类型 | 示例 | 谓语 |
|----------|------|------|
| 学科名称 | Mathematics, Physics, Economics, Politics | **Mathematics is** my favorite subject. |
| 疾病名称 | Measles, Mumps, Diabetes | **Measles is** contagious. |
| 游戏名称 | Darts, Billiards, Cards (有时) | **Darts is** a fun game. |
| 国家/组织名称 | the United States, the United Nations | **The United States is** a large country. |
| 书名/影视名 | "The Great Expectations" | **"Great Expectations" is** a classic. |
| 时间/金额/距离（整体） | Ten dollars, Five miles, Two hours | **Ten dollars is** not enough. |

### 6.2.3 不定代词的单复数意义

**定义**：有些不定代词（如 all, some, most, none, half）的谓语形式取决于它们所指代的名词是可数还是不可数。

**规则**：看 of 后面的名词（如果存在）或根据上下文判断。

| 不定代词 | 所指名词为单数/不可数 → 单数谓语 | 所指名词为复数 → 复数谓语 |
|----------|------------------------------|---------------------------|
| all of... | All of the **water is** gone. | All of the **students are** here. |
| some of... | Some of the **money was** spent. | Some of the **books are** missing. |
| most of... | Most of the **time has** passed. | Most of the **people have** left. |
| half of... | Half of the **cake was** eaten. | Half of the **apples are** rotten. |
| none of... | None of the **information is** useful. | None of the **answers are** correct. |
| the rest of... | The rest of the **water was** wasted. | The rest of the **students were** late. |

### 6.2.4 名词的形和意

#### 形复意单（用单数谓语）

**定义**：名词形式上是**复数**（以 -s 结尾），但意义上是**单数**概念，谓语用单数。

| 类型 | 示例 | 谓语 |
|------|------|------|
| 学科 | Mathematics, Physics, Linguistics | **Mathematics is** difficult. |
| 疾病 | Measles, Mumps, Herpes | **Measles has** no cure. |
| 国家/机构 | The Philippines, The United Nations | **The Philippines is** an island nation. |
| 抽象概念 | News, Statistics (作为学科) | **The news is** good. |

#### 形单意复（用复数谓语）

**定义**：名词形式上是**单数**（不以 -s 结尾），但意义上是**复数**概念，谓语用复数。

| 类型 | 示例 | 谓语 |
|------|------|------|
| 集体名词（英式用法） | family, team, class, committee, government | My **family are** coming from different cities. |
| people（人们） | people, police, cattle, poultry | **The police are** investigating. |
| 总称名词 | the rich, the poor, the young, the old | **The rich are** getting richer. |

**常用形单意复的名词**：

| 名词 | 含义 | 谓语 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|------|
| people | 人们 | 复数 | **People are** waiting outside. |
| police | 警察（全体） | 复数 | **The police have** arrived. |
| cattle | 牛（群体） | 复数 | **Cattle are** grazing. |
| poultry | 家禽 | 复数 | **Poultry are** raised on farms. |
| clergy | 神职人员 | 复数 | **The clergy are** gathered. |

> **注意**：`people` 作“民族”解时，有单复数形式：a people（一个民族）→ many peoples（许多民族），此时谓语与形式一致。

#### 单复数同形（单数/复数谓语都可）

**定义**：名词的**单数和复数形式相同**，谓语形式需根据**语境**（数量意义）来判断。

| 名词 | 单数意义（单数谓语） | 复数意义（复数谓语） |
|------|---------------------|---------------------|
| sheep | **The sheep is** lost. (一只羊) | **The sheep are** grazing. (多只羊) |
| deer | **A deer was** seen. (一只鹿) | **Deer are** common here. (鹿群) |
| fish | **The fish is** dead. (一条鱼) | **Fish are** swimming. (多条鱼) |
| species | **This species is** endangered. (一个物种) | **These species are** endangered. (多个物种) |
| series | **The series is** popular. (一个系列) | **Two series are** airing. (两个系列) |
| means | **Every means has** been tried. (一种方法) | **All means are** acceptable. (多种方法) |
| works | **The works was** closed. (一家工厂) | **The works are** old. (多部作品/多家工厂) |

#### 集合名词（单数/复数谓语都可）

**定义**：集合名词既可以表示**整体**（视为一个单位），也可以表示**成员**（视为多个个体）。

| 集合名词 | 整体（单数谓语） | 成员（复数谓语） |
|----------|-----------------|-----------------|
| family | My family **is** large. | My family **are** early risers. |
| team | The team **is** strong. | The team **are** arguing. |
| class | The class **has** 30 students. | The class **are** taking a test. |
| committee | The committee **has** decided. | The committee **are** divided. |
| government | The government **is** in power. | The government **are** discussing. |
| audience | The audience **was** huge. | The audience **were** clapping. |
| staff | The staff **is** trained. | The staff **are** working hard. |

> **美式 vs 英式**：
> - 美式英语：集合名词通常用**单数**谓语
> - 英式英语：集合名词可用单数（强调整体）或复数（强调成员）

## 6.3 就近一致

**定义**：谓语动词的单复数与**最近的主语**保持一致。这种原则主要出现在某些特定的并列结构中。

### 6.3.1 or / either...or... / neither...nor... / not only...but also...

**规则**：在这些结构中，谓语与**最近的主语**（即 or/nor/but also 后面的主语）保持一致。

| 结构 | 示例 | 谓语与谁一致 |
|------|------|--------------|
| or | You **or** he **is** responsible. | 与 he 一致 |
| either...or... | Either you **or** I **am** going. | 与 I 一致 |
| neither...nor... | Neither the teacher **nor** the students **are** happy. | 与 students 一致 |
| not only...but also... | Not only the students **but also** the teacher **is** here. | 与 teacher 一致 |

**更多示例**：

| 结构 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| or | Is it Tom **or** his brothers **who is** calling? (与 brothers 一致？注意：brothers 复数，但这里 is 与 Tom 一致？需谨慎) → 最好：Is it Tom or his brothers who **are** calling? (与最近的 brothers 一致) |
| either...or... | Either the manager **or** his assistants **are** to blame. |
| neither...nor... | Neither the employees **nor** the boss **is** happy. |
| not only...but also... | Not only the students **but also** the principal **has** arrived. |

> **注意**：为了语感流畅，建议将**复数主语**放在离谓语最近的位置。

### 6.3.2 there be 句型

**规则**：在 there be 结构中，be 动词的单复数与**最近的主语**（第一个主语）保持一致。

| 示例 | 谓语与谁一致 |
|------|--------------|
| There **is** a pen and two books on the desk. | 与 a pen 一致（单数） |
| There **are** two books and a pen on the desk. | 与 two books 一致（复数） |
| There **is** some water and three apples. | 与 some water 一致（不可数→单数） |
| There **are** three apples and some water. | 与 three apples 一致（复数） |

> **注意**：在非正式口语中，有人会违背这条规则（说 `There's two books`），但雅思写作中应严格遵守就近一致原则。

## 主谓一致速查表

### 6.1 语法一致速查

| 主语类型 | 谓语 | 示例 |
|----------|------|------|
| 可数名词单数 | 单数 | The student **is** here. |
| 可数名词复数 | 复数 | The students **are** here. |
| 不可数名词 | 单数 | Water **is** important. |
| 不定式/动名词 | 单数 | To learn **is** important. |
| 主语 + with/together with + ... | 与主语一致 | Tom with his friends **is** here. |
| A and B（不同人/物） | 复数 | Tom and Jerry **are** friends. |
| A and B（同一人/物） | 单数 | The singer and songwriter **is** here. |
| Every/Each/No + A and B | 单数 | Every boy and girl **is** here. |
| Many a + 单数名词 | 单数 | Many a student **has** tried. |
| 复合不定代词（someone等） | 单数 | Everyone **is** here. |

### 6.2 意义一致速查

| 主语类型 | 谓语 | 示例 |
|----------|------|------|
| 集合名词（整体） | 单数 | The team **is** strong. |
| 集合名词（成员） | 复数 | The team **are** arguing. |
| 学科名词（-ics） | 单数 | Mathematics **is** hard. |
| 时间/金额/距离（整体） | 单数 | Ten dollars **is** enough. |
| people/police/cattle | 复数 | The police **are** here. |
| the + 形容词（表示一类人） | 复数 | The rich **are** getting richer. |
| all/some/most/half/none + of | 看 of 后的名词 | All of the water **is** gone. / All of the students **are** here. |
| 单复数同形（sheep等） | 看语境 | The sheep **is** lost. / The sheep **are** grazing. |

### 6.3 就近一致速查

| 结构 | 谓语规则 | 示例 |
|------|----------|------|
| or / either...or... | 与最近的主语一致 | You or he **is** wrong. |
| neither...nor... | 与最近的主语一致 | Neither he nor they **are** ready. |
| not only...but also... | 与最近的主语一致 | Not only you but also I **am** going. |
| there be | 与最近的主语一致 | There **is** a pen and two books. |

## 常见错误总结

| 错误 | 正确 | 说明 |
|------|------|------|
| The teacher with his students **are** here. | The teacher with his students **is** here. | with 结构不改变主语单复数 |
| Mathematics **are** difficult. | Mathematics **is** difficult. | 学科名词用单数谓语 |
| Everyone **are** here. | Everyone **is** here. | 复合不定代词用单数谓语 |
| Ten dollars **are** too much. | Ten dollars **is** too much. | 金额视为整体，用单数谓语 |
| The police **is** coming. | The police **are** coming. | police 用复数谓语 |
| Neither you nor I **are** wrong. | Neither you nor I **am** wrong. | 就近一致：与 I 一致 |
| There **are** a pen and two books. | There **is** a pen and two books. | 就近一致：与 a pen 一致 |
| Either he or they **is** wrong. | Either he or they **are** wrong. | 就近一致：与 they 一致 |

## 雅思考试中的主谓一致应用

1. **写作 Task 1 & Task 2**：
   - **最常见错误**：主语与谓语不一致（尤其是长主语后忘记谓语单复数）
   - **提分关键**：正确使用主谓一致是语法得分的基础
   - **特别注意**：
     - 集合名词的处理（美式多用单数，英式可用复数）
     - 形复意单的名词（Mathematics is...）
     - 带有介词短语的长主语（The quality of the products **is**...）

2. **阅读部分**：
   - 通过主谓一致判断主语是谁（尤其在长难句中）
   - 识别倒装句中的主语（找出真正的主语）

3. **口语部分**：
   - 注意 everyone/anybody 等词后谓语用单数
   - There be 句型中注意 be 动词的选择

4. **高频考点**：
   - `The number of + 复数名词` → 谓语**单数**（......的数量）
   - `A number of + 复数名词` → 谓语**复数**（许多......）
   - 对比：The number of students **is** increasing. / A number of students **are** absent.

# 7 英语标点

**定义**：标点符号是书面语中用来表示**停顿、语气、句子结构**和**语义关系**的符号系统。正确使用标点符号是雅思写作的基本要求。

**标点分类总览**：

| 标点 | 符号 | 主要作用 |
|------|------|----------|
| 逗号 | , | 分隔、列举、断句 |
| 句号 | . | 结束陈述句 |
| 分号 | ; | 连接紧密相关的分句 |
| 冒号 | : | 引出解释、列举、说明 |
| 引号 | " " / ' ' | 引用、强调、讽刺 |
| 破折号 | — | 强调、转折、插入 |
| 问号 | ? | 结束疑问句 |
| 感叹号 | ! | 表达强烈情感 |
| 连字符 | - | 连接复合词 |
| 撇号 | ' | 所有格、缩写 |
| 省略号 | ... | 省略、语气延长 |

---

## 7.1 逗号（详细）

**定义**：逗号是英语中最常用、最容易出错的标点符号。它表示**最短的停顿**，用于分隔句子中的不同成分，使句子结构清晰、意义明确。

### 7.1.1 断句

**定义**：在长句中，逗号用于在**自然停顿处**断开句子，帮助读者理解句子结构，避免歧义。

**核心规则**：当一个句子超过一定长度（通常15-20个词），或含有多个修饰成分时，应在适当位置加逗号帮助断句。

| 场景 | 示例 | 说明 |
|------|------|------|
| 长主语后 | **The man who came to see me yesterday,** is my uncle. | 长主语后用逗号分隔（也可不加） |
| 状语从句后 | **After I finished my homework,** I went to bed. | 状语从句在主句前时，用逗号分隔 |
| 短语后 | **To be honest,** I don't like it. | 评论性短语后加逗号 |
| 避免歧义 | **When I finished eating,** the dog started barking. | 不加逗号可能误读为"我吃完狗" |

**对比歧义**：
- ❌ `When I finished eating the dog started barking.`（歧义：吃狗？）
- ✅ `When I finished eating, the dog started barking.`（清晰：吃完后，狗开始叫）

> **核心原则**：如果句子的自然停顿处不加逗号会导致误读，则必须加逗号。

### 7.1.2 列举

**定义**：在列出三个或以上项目时，用逗号分隔各个项目。

**核心规则**：
- 两个项目：不用逗号，直接用 `and` 或 `or`
- 三个及以上项目：用逗号分隔，最后一个项目前加 `and` 或 `or`

| 数量 | 示例 | 说明 |
|------|------|------|
| 两个 | I like **coffee and tea**. | 不用逗号 |
| 三个 | I like **coffee, tea, and juice**. | 逗号分隔 + 最后一个 and |
| 四个 | She bought **apples, bananas, oranges, and grapes**. | 同上 |

**牛津逗号（序列逗号）**：

**定义**：在列举时，最后一个项目前的 `and` 或 `or` 之前的逗号，称为牛津逗号（Oxford Comma）或序列逗号（Serial Comma）。

| 使用牛津逗号 | 不使用牛津逗号 | 说明 |
|--------------|----------------|------|
| I love my parents, **Taylor Swift,** and God. | I love my parents, **Taylor Swift and God**. | 不加逗号：父母是Taylor Swift和上帝？ |
| We invited the dancers, **Kennedy,** and Trump. | We invited the dancers, **Kennedy and Trump**. | 不加逗号：舞者是肯尼迪和特朗普？ |

> **雅思提示**：牛津逗号在美式英语中更常见，英式英语中常省略。为避免歧义，**推荐使用牛津逗号**。雅思写作中两种均可，但需保持全文一致。

### 7.1.3 同位语

**定义**：同位语是对名词或代词进行**解释、说明或补充**的成分。当同位语提供**非必要信息**时，前后用逗号隔开；当同位语提供**必要信息**时，不用逗号。

**核心区分**：
- **非限制性同位语**（非必要，删去后句子意思仍然完整）→ 加逗号
- **限制性同位语**（必要，删去后句子意思不完整或改变）→ 不加逗号

| 类型 | 示例 | 说明 |
|------|------|------|
| 非限制性 | **Beijing, the capital of China,** is a big city. | "the capital of China"是补充信息，删去后Beijing仍是Beijing |
| 非限制性 | My brother, **Tom,** is a doctor. | 只有一个brother，Tom是补充说明 |
| 限制性 | My brother **Tom** is a doctor. | 有多个brother，Tom是特指哪一个 |
| 非限制性 | **The Great Wall, one of the Seven Wonders,** attracts many tourists. | 补充说明 |
| 限制性 | The novel **"Pride and Prejudice"** is a classic. | 特指哪本小说 |

> **雅思写作提示**：非限制性同位语（加逗号）是增加句子复杂度的好方法，但不要过度使用。

### 7.1.4 引语

**定义**：在直接引语中，用逗号分隔**引述句**（如 he said, she asked）和**引用的内容**。

**核心规则**：
- 引述句在前：引述句后加逗号，引语首字母大写
- 引语在前：引语后加逗号（在引号内），引述句首字母小写
- 引语被分隔：引语第一部分后加逗号（在引号内），引述句后加逗号

| 位置 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| 引述句在前 | He said, **"I will come tomorrow."** |
| 引语在前 | **"I will come tomorrow,"** he said. |
| 引语被分隔 | **"I will come,"** he said, **"if I have time."** |

**与句号的配合**：
- 引语结束且引述句在后：引语末尾用逗号（在引号内），不用句号
- 引语结束且句子结束：句号放在引号**外面**（英式）或**里面**（美式）

> **雅思提示**：雅思写作中引语较少使用。如使用，注意引述句后的逗号和引号内外的标点规则。

### 7.1.5 插入语

**定义**：插入语是插入句子中的**额外信息**（如副词、短语、评论），通常与句子其他部分用逗号隔开。

**常见插入语类型**：

| 类型 | 示例 | 说明 |
|------|------|------|
| 评论性副词 | **However,** I disagree. / This is, **of course,** true. | however, therefore, moreover, consequently |
| 插入短语 | The test, **in my opinion,** is too hard. | in fact, for example, on the contrary |
| 插入从句 | The man, **I believe,** is innocent. | I think, I suppose, you know |
| 插入名字/称呼 | **Well,** let's go. / **Tom,** come here. | well, yes, no, oh |

**核心规则**：插入语前后各加一个逗号（如果位于句首，则只在后面加逗号）。

| 位置 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| 句首 | **However,** he passed the test. |
| 句中 | He, **however,** passed the test. |
| 句尾 | He passed the test, **however.** |

> **注意**：`however` 作插入语时前后加逗号；作连词（=but）时，前面加分号或句号，后面加逗号。`It was late; however, we continued.`

### 7.1.6 并列句与复杂句

#### 并列句中的逗号

**定义**：由**并列连词**（and, but, or, for, nor, so, yet）连接的两个独立分句时，在连词**前**加逗号。

**结构公式**：`分句1 + , + 并列连词 + 分句2`

| 示例 | 说明 |
|------|------|
| I like coffee, **but** she likes tea. | 两个完整分句 |
| It was raining, **so** we stayed home. | 两个完整分句 |
| She studied hard, **and** she passed the exam. | 两个完整分句 |

**何时可以省略逗号**：如果两个分句**很短**且**关系紧密**，可以省略逗号。

| 加逗号 | 不加逗号 | 说明 |
|--------|----------|------|
| I came, and I saw. | I came and I saw. | 短句可不加 |
| She ran, and he followed. | She ran and he followed. | 短句可不加 |

> **注意**：如果主语相同且第二个分句省略主语，则不加逗号：`He came and (he) sat down.`

#### 复杂句中的逗号

**定义**：当**从句**（状语从句、非限制性定语从句）位于主句**之前**时，从句后加逗号；位于主句**之后**时，通常不加逗号。

| 从句位置 | 示例 | 说明 |
|----------|------|------|
| 从句在前 | **When I arrived,** he was already there. | 从句后加逗号 |
| 从句在后 | He was already there **when I arrived.** | 不加逗号 |
| 原因从句在前 | **Because it was raining,** we stayed home. | 从句后加逗号 |
| 原因从句在后 | We stayed home **because it was raining.** | 不加逗号 |
| 让步从句在前 | **Although he was tired,** he kept working. | 从句后加逗号 |
| 让步从句在后 | He kept working **although he was tired.** | 不加逗号 |

**非限制性定语从句**（无论位置，都用逗号）：

| 类型 | 示例 | 说明 |
|------|------|------|
| 非限制性 | My father, **who is a doctor,** works in London. | 前后逗号 |
| 限制性 | The man **who is a doctor** is my father. | 不用逗号 |

**对比**：
- 限制性（不用逗号）：`The students who studied hard passed.`（只有学习刻苦的学生通过了）
- 非限制性（用逗号）：`The students, who studied hard, passed.`（所有学生都学习了，他们都通过了）

### 7.1.7 附加疑问句

**定义**：附加疑问句（反义疑问句）中，陈述句部分与疑问尾句之间用逗号分隔。

**结构公式**：`陈述句 + , + 疑问尾句？`

| 示例 | 说明 |
|------|------|
| You like coffee, **don't you?** | 肯定陈述 + 否定尾句 |
| You don't like tea, **do you?** | 否定陈述 + 肯定尾句 |
| He is a teacher, **isn't he?** | be动词 |
| She can swim, **can't she?** | 情态动词 |

> **注意**：疑问尾句的首字母**不大写**（除非尾句以 I 开头）。

---

## 7.2 句号

**定义**：句号表示**句子的结束**，用于陈述句和祈使句的末尾。

**核心用法**：

| 用法 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| 结束陈述句 | The sun rises in the east. |
| 结束祈使句 | Please close the door. |
| 用于缩写（部分） | Mr., Dr., e.g., i.e. |

**注意事项**：
- 英文句号是 **.**（点），不是中文的 **。**（圈）
- 句号后空一格再接下一句（雅思写作标准格式）
- 句子以缩写结尾（如 `etc.`），句号兼作结尾句号，不需要再加一个。

---

## 7.3 分号

**定义**：分号表示比逗号**更长**、比句号**更短**的停顿，用于连接**关系紧密**的两个独立分句。

**核心用法**：

| 用法 | 示例 | 说明 |
|------|------|------|
| 连接相关分句（无连词） | I love coffee; my wife loves tea. | 两个分句关系紧密 |
| 与连接副词连用 | It was late; **however,** we continued. | 分号 + 连接副词 + 逗号 |
| 分隔含有逗号的列举项 | We visited Paris, France; London, UK; and Rome, Italy. | 列举项内部有逗号 |

**分号 vs 句号**：
- 句号：完全分隔两个句子，独立性更强
- 分号：两个分句关系更紧密，通常共同表达一个完整意思

---

## 7.4 冒号

**定义**：冒号用于**引出**对前面内容的解释、列举、说明或引用。

**核心用法**：

| 用法 | 示例 | 说明 |
|------|------|------|
| 引出列举 | I need three things: money, time, and luck. | 冒号前必须是完整句子 |
| 引出解释 | He had one goal: to win. | 解释前面的内容 |
| 引出引用 | The speaker said: "We shall overcome." | 正式引用 |
| 引出例子 | There is one solution: work harder. | 解释解决方案 |

**冒号 vs 分号**：
- 冒号：后面的内容**解释或补充**前面的内容
- 分号：前后两个分句**并列平等**

---

## 7.5 引号

**定义**：引号用于标示**直接引语**、**特殊词汇**或**作品标题**。

**核心用法**：

| 用法 | 示例 | 说明 |
|------|------|------|
| 直接引语 | She said, "I'm tired." | 引用原话 |
| 特殊词汇/讽刺 | His "help" made things worse. | 表示讽刺或特殊含义 |
| 作品标题（短篇） | I read "The Tell-Tale Heart." | 短篇作品用引号，长篇用斜体 |

**英式 vs 美式**：
- 美式：双引号 `" "` 在外，单引号 `' '` 在内
- 英式：单引号 `' '` 在外，双引号 `" "` 在内

**标点位置**：
- 美式：句号/逗号放在引号**里面**
- 英式：句号/逗号放在引号**外面**（除非是引语的一部分）

---

## 7.6 破折号

**定义**：破折号表示**强调、转折、插入或列举**，语气比逗号强，比括号更醒目。

**核心用法**：

| 用法 | 示例 | 说明 |
|------|------|------|
| 强调插入 | The answer — surprisingly — was yes. | 强调插入内容 |
| 解释说明 | He has one passion — music. | 破折号后面的内容强烈突出 |
| 列举总结 | Coffee, tea, juice — all are available. | 对前面的列举进行总结 |
| 语气转折 | I wanted to go — but it was too late. | 表示转折 |

**破折号 vs 逗号 vs 括号**：
- 逗号：最温和的插入
- 括号：最次要的插入（可忽略的信息）
- 破折号：最强的插入（强调信息）

> **注意**：英文破折号通常用**两个连字符** `--` 或在Word中自动生成的 **—**（em dash），前后**不加空格**。

---

## 7.7 问号、感叹号

**问号**：

| 用法 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| 直接疑问句 | What is your name? |
| 反义疑问句 | You like coffee, don't you? |
| 表示疑问语气（不一定是问句） | You did what? |

> **注意**：间接疑问句**不用问号**：`He asked what my name was.`

**感叹号**：

| 用法 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| 强烈情感 | What a beautiful day! |
| 命令/警告 | Stop! |
| 惊讶/感叹 | I can't believe it! |

> **雅思提示**：学术写作中**很少使用感叹号**，雅思写作应尽量**避免**使用。

---

## 7.8 连字符、撇号

**连字符**：

| 用法 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| 合成词 | mother-in-law, good-looking, well-known |
| 数字/分数 | twenty-one, two-thirds |
| 前缀后（有时） | pre-war, ex-president |

**撇号**：

| 用法 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| 所有格（名词） | Tom's book, the students' books, children's toys |
| 缩写 | don't, it's (it is), I'm, they've |
| 省略数字 | the '90s (1990s) |

> **注意**：`it's` = it is / it has；`its` = 所有格（它的）。不要混淆。

---

## 7.9 省略号

**定义**：省略号表示**省略内容**、**语气延长**或**犹豫**。

**核心用法**：

| 用法 | 示例 | 说明 |
|------|------|------|
| 省略引用内容 | "He said... it was important." | 引用中省略无关内容 |
| 语气延长 | I'm not sure... maybe... | 犹豫或思考 |
| 未完成句子 | I was going to say... never mind. | 句子未完成 |

> **雅思提示**：学术写作中**避免使用省略号**，除非是直接引用需要省略内容。

## 雅思考试中的标点注意事项

1. **写作 Task 1 & Task 2**：
   - **逗号是最重要的标点**，90%的标点使用都是逗号
   - 切忌用逗号连接两个独立分句（逗号拼接错误）
     - ❌ `It was late, we went home.`
     - ✅ `It was late, **so** we went home.` / `It was late **.** We went home.`
   - 句号后空一格，不要连续使用多个空格
   - 避免使用感叹号和省略号
   - 分号和冒号适当使用可以提升句式多样性

2. **常见错误**：
   - 逗号拼接（comma splice）：两个分句之间只用逗号，没有连词
   - 不加逗号导致歧义：`When I finished eating the dog started barking.`
   - 在限制性定语从句中误加逗号
   - 引号内外的标点位置错误（英式/美式混用）

3. **书写规范**：
   - 标点符号后空一格（句号、逗号、冒号、分号后）
   - 标点符号前不空格（破折号除外，破折号前后不加空格）
   - 括号/引号内侧不加空格：`( like this )` ❌
