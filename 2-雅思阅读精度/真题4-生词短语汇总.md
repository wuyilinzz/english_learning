#### 短语

| 短语 | 意思 | 例句 |
|---|---|---|
| be confronted with sth | 被动固定搭配 = 被迫遭遇、面临 | We are confronted with difficulties. |
| alarming rate | 惊人速度（这两单词合一起不知道怎么翻译） | His money was running out at an alarming rate/speed. |
| in the face of | 面对 | We won the contract in the face of stiff(激烈的) competition. |
| be consistent with | 与什么一致 | We do not consider his behaviour to be consistent with the holding of a high-ranking job.我们认为他的行为与他位居要职的身份不相符。 |
| place...on sth | 把...放在...上面 | Please place the book on the table.请把书放在桌上。 |
| - be responsible for sth/sb/doing sth<br>- be responsible to sb/sth | 对某事/某人负责 | - The defendant was depressed and therefore not fully responsible for her own actions.<br>- In Australia, the prime minister and the Cabinet are responsible to the House of Representatives. |
| take sth/sb for granted | 把某事/某物想当然 或 不珍惜某人、把某人的付出当理所应当 | Don't take your health for granted. |
